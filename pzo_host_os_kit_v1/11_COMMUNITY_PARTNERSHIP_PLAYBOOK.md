# Point Zero One Digital Community Partnership Playbook

## Overview

This playbook outlines the strategy for church/campus/coworking partnerships, including the Play-to-Prosper Night pitch script, host kit licensing, facilitator training path, Play-to-Prosper Pledge integration, and donation box mechanics.

## Non-Negotiables

1. Strict adherence to Point Zero One Digital's values: production-grade, deployment-ready, deterministic effects, and strict TypeScript mode with no 'any'.
2. Partnerships must align with our mission and vision.
3. All events must be well-organized and professionally executed.
4. Transparency in financial transactions and donations.
5. Respect for the intellectual property rights of Point Zero One Digital.

## Implementation Spec

### Play-to-Prosper Night Pitch Script

1. Introduction: Briefly introduce Point Zero One Digital, our mission, and the purpose of the event.
2. Game Demonstration: Showcase the gameplay of Point Zero One Digital's 12-minute financial roguelike game.
3. Partnership Opportunities: Explain how the church/campus/coworking space can partner with us to host Play-to-Prosper Nights.
4. Q&A Session: Allow time for questions and answers.

### Host Kit Licensing

1. License Agreement: Provide a clear, concise license agreement outlining the terms of use for the host kit.
2. Kit Contents: Include all necessary materials for hosting a successful Play-to-Prosper Night, such as promotional materials, game download links, and facilitator guides.
3. Renewal Process: Establish a simple process for renewing the license agreement when needed.

### Facilitator Training Path

1. Online Training Modules: Offer self-paced online training modules to equip facilitators with the knowledge and skills necessary to host successful Play-to-Prosper Nights.
2. Live Webinars: Host live webinars for Q&A sessions and additional training opportunities.
3. Certification Program: Implement a certification program for top performers, providing them with special recognition and potential future opportunities.

### Play-to-Prosper Pledge Integration

1. Pledge Form: Provide a pledge form for attendees to commit to supporting Point Zero One Digital's mission financially.
2. Transparency: Clearly communicate how donations will be used and ensure transparency in financial transactions.
3. Incentives: Offer incentives, such as exclusive merchandise or early access to new games, for higher-tier pledges.

### Donation Box Mechanics

1. Physical Donation Boxes: Provide physical donation boxes at events for attendees to make cash donations.
2. Online Donation Platform: Offer an online donation platform for those who cannot attend events or prefer digital transactions.
3. Tax Receipts: Issue tax receipts for all donations over a certain threshold, as required by law.

## Edge Cases

1. Event Cancellation: In the event of cancellation, provide clear communication to partners and attendees regarding refunds or rescheduling options.
2. Donation Mishaps: Implement a system for handling and reporting any issues related to donations, such as lost or misplaced funds.
3. License Agreement Violations: Establish a process for addressing violations of the license agreement, including termination of partnerships if necessary.
