Day 3 follow-up

Have you run your first night yet?

By day 3, the move is simple: don’t overthink the perfect event. Run the first night, keep the structure tight, and leave with a reason to run it again.

If you haven’t hosted yet, use the shortest version: one warm start, one full run, one three-question debrief, and one next-night booking before people walk out.

Moment code reminder

HOS-A01 — Opportunity Flip

Use this when someone turns a risky decision into a momentum swing and the whole table feels it.
 

HOS-B01 — FUBAR Killed Me

Use this when chaos lands hard, the room erupts, and one card instantly changes the emotional temperature.
 

HOS-C01 — Missed the Bag

Use this when someone realizes the smarter move too late and the table immediately starts rewriting history.

Debrief prompt sample

Keep the debrief short. Three prompts. Then stop while people still want one more run.

1) What was your best decision tonight? 2) What was your worst decision tonight? 3) If you got one do-over, where would you use it? Optional reveal prompt: What did this game show you about your risk tolerance? Close with: Same time next week. Pick the theme now. Each person brings one person.

Fast win

Your first night does not need perfect attendance, perfect lighting, or perfect footage. It needs structure, one memorable swing, one clean debrief, and the next date locked before dopamine fades.
