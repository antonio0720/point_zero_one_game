Night 2 checklist

Night 2 is where a one-off event starts becoming a pattern. The goal is not to do more. The goal is to repeat what worked, tighten what dragged, and give the table something to talk about after they leave.

Open Night 2 Dashboard Join Host OS v2 Waitlist

Checklist before people arrive
□ Pick tonight’s format: Casual Table, Community Night, or Competitive Night. □ Pick tonight’s theme: Risk Night, Speed Night, Betrayal Night, or Recovery Night. □ Assign the four roles: Timer, Dealer, Clipper, Scorekeeper. □ Cap capture at 3–10 moments total. □ Decide what counts as the “must-post” clip before the first draw. □ End the night by booking the next one on the spot.

3 moment codes to try tonight

HOS-A01 — Opportunity Flip

Log this when someone threads the needle and the whole table instantly knows the game just changed.
 

HOS-B01 — FUBAR Killed Me

Log this when a brutal reversal lands and someone says some version of “I was fine 30 seconds ago.”
 

HOS-C01 — Missed the Bag

Log this when hindsight hits hard and everyone starts explaining the smarter move after it’s already too late.

Simple release workflow

Don’t try to publish everything. Ship the cleanest few artifacts.

Post 1: best moment clip Post 2: best reaction clip Post 3: one insight line the table would actually repeat Caption formula: “What looked like a smart move became a disaster in under a minute.”
