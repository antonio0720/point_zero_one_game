3 tips for your first night

1) Pick one format before people arrive.

Run the night as a Casual Table, Community Night, or Competitive Night. The format answers “what kind of night is this?” before confusion starts.

 

2) Assign roles in under 60 seconds.

Give someone Timer, someone Dealer, someone Clipper, and someone Scorekeeper. Shared roles reduce host fatigue and increase buy-in fast.

 

3) End with the next-night contract.

Before people leave, lock the next date, pick the next theme, and challenge each player to bring one person. That one habit is what turns “fun night” into “weekly ritual.”

Host reminder

Your goal is not to capture everything. Your goal is to capture the few moments people will quote tomorrow.

Aim for 3 moment clips, 1 reaction clip, and 1 insight line. Stop there. The best host nights are sustainable.
