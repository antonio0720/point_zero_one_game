Crafting Progression - Level 5
=============================

Overview
--------

Level 5 of the crafting progression introduces new materials, recipes, and challenges to test your skills as a crafter. With increased complexity come greater rewards, but be prepared for tougher obstacles on your path to mastery.

Materials
----------

### Level 5 Materials

- Rare Ore (Level 4 or higher)
- Advanced Alloys (Level 4 or higher)
- Precious Gems (Level 4 or higher)
- Exotic Leathers (Level 4 or higher)
- Mysterious Artifacts (New, can only be found at Level 5 or higher zones)

### New Materials

*Mysterious Artifacts* are a rare and valuable resource that can be found exclusively in Level 5 and above zones. They have unique properties and are essential for crafting high-end items. Be sure to salvage any artifacts you find, as they can provide valuable components for your projects.

Recipes
-------

### Level 5 Crafting Recipes

1. **Advanced Weapon X**: Requires Rare Ore (x3), Advanced Alloys (x2), Precious Gems (x1), Exotic Leather (x1), and a Mysterious Artifact (x1). This weapon offers superior stats compared to previous tiers, making it an ideal choice for seasoned adventurers.

2. **Durable Armor Y**: Requires Rare Ore (x4), Advanced Alloys (x3), Precious Gems (x2), Exotic Leather (x2), and a Mysterious Artifact (x1). This armor provides excellent protection against enemy attacks, making it an essential piece of equipment for those venturing into dangerous territory.

Challenges
----------

### Level 5 Crafting Challenges

1. **Artisan's Triumph**: Craft a set of Advanced Weapon X and Durable Armor Y within a given time frame. Rewards include bonus crafting experience, rare materials, and an exclusive title.

2. **Mystery Box Mastery**: Salvage 50 Mysterious Artifacts. Rewards include increased chances to find high-quality artifacts, as well as additional crafting experience points.

3. **Elite Enigma**: Craft a weapon or armor using only exotic materials and no common components. This challenge tests your ability to create powerful items from limited resources.

### Level 5 Crafting Achievements

1. **The Artisan's Touch**: Craft 50 Advanced Weapon X or Durable Armor Y. Rewards include increased crafting speed, unique cosmetic items, and an exclusive title.

2. **Mystery Solved**: Salvage 100 Mysterious Artifacts. Rewards include a powerful artifact as well as additional crafting experience points.

3. **Resourceful Recycler**: Salvage every exotic material available at Level 5 or higher. Rewards include an exclusive title, increased chances to find rare materials, and additional crafting experience points.
