Crafting Progression - Level 6
=============================

Welcome to the sixth level of crafting progression! In this level, you will unlock new recipes and improve your crafting abilities.

New Recipes
------------

1. **Advanced Tool**
- Materials: 5 Iron Ore + 2 Coal
- Crafting Time: 3 minutes
- Description: A more durable and efficient tool made from stronger materials.

2. **Iron Sword**
- Materials: 7 Iron Ore + 3 Coal
- Crafting Time: 5 minutes
- Description: A sword forged from iron, providing a good balance between durability and damage.

3. **Iron Armor Set (Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots)**
- Materials: 18 Iron Ore + 9 Coal
- Crafting Time: 10 minutes
- Description: A set of armor made from iron, offering improved protection compared to its wooden counterpart.

Progression Rewards
--------------------

Upon reaching level 6 in crafting, you will receive the following rewards:

- XP Bonus: +5% experience points gained from crafting
- Inventory Space: +20 slots
- Crafting Speed: +10% faster crafting speed

Remember to keep upgrading your workbench to unlock even more recipes and improve your crafting abilities further. Happy crafting!
