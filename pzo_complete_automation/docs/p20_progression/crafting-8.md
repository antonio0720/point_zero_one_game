Crafting-8
==========

Crafting Level 8 introduces new and challenging recipes to create powerful items. As you progress, you'll unlock more complex and impressive craftable gear.

### New Recipes

#### Weapons
1. Rare Sword - Requires 5 Iron Swords and 3 Crystals of Power. Creates a weapon with increased attack power.
2. Epic Dagger - Requires 7 Silver Daggers and 5 Shards of Light. Crafts a dagger that deals more damage than its silver counterpart.
3. Legendary Mace - Requires 10 Stone Maces, 7 Crystals of Strength, and 2 Mysterious Essences. Creates a powerful mace with additional effects.

#### Armors
1. Rare Helmet - Requires 5 Iron Helmets and 3 Crystals of Resilience. Crafts a helmet that improves resistance to damage.
2. Epic Chestplate - Requires 7 Silver Chestplates, 5 Shards of Defense, and 2 Mysterious Essences. Creates an armor piece with improved defense.
3. Legendary Boots - Requires 10 Stone Boots, 7 Crystals of Speed, and 2 Mysterious Essences. Crafts boots that increase movement speed.

#### Consumables
1. Elixir of Strength - Requires 5 Crystals of Power and 3 Common Herbs. Increases attack power for a short duration.
2. Elixir of Defense - Requires 5 Crystals of Resilience and 3 Common Herbs. Improves resistance to damage for a short duration.
3. Elixir of Speed - Requires 5 Crystals of Speed and 3 Common Herbs. Increases movement speed for a short duration.

### Progression

To reach Crafting Level 8, you must first achieve the following milestones:

1. Master Crafting-7: Complete all Crafting-7 recipes.
2. Gather Resources: Collect enough materials to craft 50 items from previous levels.
3. Achievements Unlocked: Earn a total of 5 Crafting-related achievements.
