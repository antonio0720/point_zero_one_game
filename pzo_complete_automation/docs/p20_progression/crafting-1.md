Crafting Progression - Part 1
==========================

Overview
--------

This document outlines the progression and achievements for the crafting system in our game. The first part covers the initial stages of learning and mastering basic crafting techniques.

Learning to Craft
------------------

### Prerequisites

1. Reach level 5 in the desired crafting skill (e.g., Blacksmithing, Alchemy, Tailoring)
2. Complete the introductory questline for the chosen crafting profession

### Achievements

1. **Crafting Apprentice**: Successfully complete the initial questline for a crafting profession and learn your first basic recipes.
2. **First Forge (Blacksmithing)**, **First Loom (Tailoring)**, **First Bottle (Alchemy)** - Craft your first item using each of the available crafting professions.
3. **Toolmaster**: Craft a set of basic tools for each profession (e.g., Smith's Hammer, Tailor's Scissors, Alchemist's Mortar and Pestle).
4. **Recipe Collector**: Gather a specified number of unique crafting recipes from various sources (e.g., quest rewards, vendors, discovery).

### Progression

1. Level up your chosen crafting profession to learn new recipes and improve crafting skill level.
2. Increase your proficiency in each crafting profession by practicing and experimenting with different materials and recipes.
3. Explore the world for rare crafting materials that can be used to create powerful, unique items.

Mastery Rewards
---------------

Upon reaching a high level of mastery in a crafting profession, players will unlock special rewards such as:

1. Exclusive craftable items with enhanced stats and effects
2. The ability to create rare or mythical quality items
3. Access to secret crafting recipes hidden throughout the world
4. Titles and achievements that reflect your mastery in the chosen profession (e.g., Master Blacksmith, Grandmaster Tailor)

Upcoming Features
-----------------

In future updates, we plan to add additional crafting professions, as well as improvements to the existing system, such as:

1. The introduction of new recipes and materials that require higher level crafting skills to create
2. Crafting challenges and events that offer exclusive rewards for participants
3. A system for player-to-player trading and auction house sales of crafted items
4. Collaborative crafting projects, where multiple players work together to create powerful or unique items.
