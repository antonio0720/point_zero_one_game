Crafting Progression (Part 2)
---------------------------

### Overview

This document outlines the crafting system's advancement in Part 2 of our game, including achievements and progression details.

### Crafting System Improvements

1. **Expanded Recipe Library**: More recipes will be added to the game, including new weapons, armor, consumables, and decorative items.

2. **Crafting Tiers**: New crafting tiers will become available as players progress through the game, allowing for more advanced and powerful items to be created.

3. **Special Resources**: Unique resources will be introduced that can only be obtained from specific enemies or rare events. These resources will be essential for crafting high-tier items.

### Crafting Achievements

1. **Jack of All Trades** - Reach level 5 in all crafting professions.

2. **Master Smith** - Reach the maximum level in Blacksmithing.

3. **Enchanter Supreme** - Reach the maximum level in Enchanting.

4. **Alchemist Extraordinaire** - Reach the maximum level in Alchemy.

5. **Artisan's Legacy** - Craft and wear a full set of artisan-crafted armor.

6. **The Armorer's Challenge** - Craft and wield a legendary weapon.

7. **The Collector** - Gather every resource required for crafting in the game.

8. **The Recipe Hunter** - Discover every recipe in the game.

### Progression Rewards

1. Leveling up in a crafting profession will reward experience points that can be spent on various bonuses, such as increased success chance or resource efficiency.

2. Reaching certain milestones (e.g., every 5 levels) will grant unique items or bonuses specific to that crafting profession.

3. Completing a crafting achievement will reward the player with a special title and a unique cosmetic item related to that achievement.

4. Players who collect every resource required for crafting in the game will unlock an exclusive, powerful crafting tool that can only be used by these players.

### Future Developments

1. **Crafting Quests**: Special quests focused on crafting will be introduced, providing players with new challenges and rewards.

2. **Community Workshops**: Players will have the ability to create and manage their own workshops, where other players can commission items for a fee.

3. **PvP Crafting Events**: Competitive events centered around crafting will be held periodically, allowing players to test their skills against one another.

4. **Guild Crafting Challenges**: Guilds will be able to participate in crafting challenges, with the winning guild receiving special rewards.
