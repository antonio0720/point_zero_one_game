Crafting Progression - Level 7
=============================

Level up your crafting skills to level 7 and unlock new recipes, materials, and achievements.

Recipes
-------

### Common Recipes (1000 Crafting Experience)

1. Basic Iron Sword: Requires 5 Iron Ingots, 3 Leather Strips, and 1 Wooden Handle.
2. Stone Pickaxe: Requires 8 Cobblestone Blocks, 2 Sticks, and 1 Iron Ingot.
3. Torch (Wood): Requires 1 Wood Plank, 1 Coal, and 1 Stick.
4. Arrow (Flint): Requires 1 Flint, 1 Feather, and 3 String.
5. Basic Leather Armor: Requires 9 Leather Strips and 3 Cobblestone Blocks.
6. Furnace: Requires 8 Cobblestone Blocks, 4 Obsidian, and 2 Blaze Rods.

### Uncommon Recipes (1500 Crafting Experience)

1. Iron Pickaxe: Requires 3 Iron Ingots and 2 Sticks.
2. Torch (Coal): Requires 1 Coal, 1 Stick, and 1 Charcoal.
3. Arrow (Feather): Requires 1 Feather, 1 Flint, and 3 String.
4. Basic Iron Chestplate: Requires 9 Iron Ingots.
5. Stone Shovel: Requires 3 Cobblestone Blocks, 2 Sticks, and 1 Iron Ingot.
6. Stone Sword: Requires 2 Iron Ingots, 2 Sticks, and 4 Cobblestone Blocks.
7. Diamond Pickaxe (Recipe Unlock): Requires 3 Diamonds, 2 Iron Ingots, and 1 Stick.
8. Enchanted Book (Recipe Unlock): Requires 1 Book, 1 Lapis Lazuli, and 1 Redstone Dust.

### Achievements

#### Basic Crafting

* **Beginner Blacksmith**: Craft a Basic Iron Sword.
* **Torch Bearer**: Craft 5 Torches (Wood) or more.
* **Arrow Maker**: Craft 10 Arrows (Flint) or more.
* **Leathercrafter**: Craft a Basic Leather Armor set (Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, and Boots).
* **Furnace Builder**: Build and light a Furnace.

#### Advanced Crafting

* **Stonecutter**: Craft a Stone Pickaxe or more.
* **Lightbringer**: Craft 5 Torches (Coal) or more.
* **Feathered Archer**: Craft 10 Arrows (Feather) or more.
* **Iron Armor Collector**: Collect and craft an Iron Chestplate, Helmet, Leggings, and Boots.
* **Stone Shaper**: Craft a Stone Shovel or more.
* **Iron Swordsman**: Craft a Stone Sword or more.
* **Diamond Seeker**: Unlock the Diamond Pickaxe recipe.
* **Book of Magic**: Unlock the Enchanted Book recipe.
