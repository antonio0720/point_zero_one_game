Crafting Progression - Level 10
=============================

In this document, you will find the details about the achievements and progression related to crafting at level 10.

Achievements
------------

1. **Master Smith**: Reach level 10 in the crafting profession.
- Reward: 1000 Crafting Experience, +5% increase in crafting speed.

2. **Exquisite Creations**: Create a combination of 10 exquisite items from any crafting discipline.
- Reward: 750 Crafting Experience, +3% increase in crafting success rate.

3. **Diverse Artisan**: Successfully complete at least one project from each crafting discipline.
- Reward: 500 Crafting Experience, +2% increase in resource gathering speed.

Progression
-----------

At level 10, you will unlock the following abilities and recipes:

### Blacksmithing
- **Tier 3**: Recipes for crafting armor and weapons of superior quality.
- **Smithing II**: Increase your chances of creating superior items.
- **Crafted Armor Reinforcement I**: Reinforce your armor for increased protection.

### Tailoring
- **Tier 3**: Recipes for crafting clothes and bags of superior quality.
- **Sewing II**: Increase your chances of creating superior items.
- **Crafted Clothing Reinforcement I**: Reinforce your clothing for increased protection.

### Leatherworking
- **Tier 3**: Recipes for crafting armor and bags of superior quality from leather.
- **Leatherworking II**: Increase your chances of creating superior items.
- **Crafted Leather Armor Reinforcement I**: Reinforce your leather armor for increased protection.

### Alchemy
- **Tier 3**: Recipes for crafting potions and elixirs of superior quality.
- **Alchemical Formulations II**: Increase your chances of creating superior potions.

### Enchanting
- **Tier 3**: Recipes for enchanting items of superior quality.
- **Enchanter's Insight II**: Increase your chances of creating superior enchantments.

### Jewelcrafting
- **Tier 3**: Recipes for crafting gems and jewelry of superior quality.
- **Jewelcrafting II**: Increase your chances of creating superior gems.

### Engineering
- **Tier 3**: Recipes for crafting gadgets, weapons, and trinkets of superior quality.
- **Engineering II**: Increase your chances of creating superior devices.

### Cooking
- **Tier 3**: Recipes for cooking dishes of superior quality.
- **Culinary Mastery II**: Increase your chances of creating superior dishes.
