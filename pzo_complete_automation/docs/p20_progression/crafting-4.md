Crafting 4
==========

This section covers the fourth stage of the crafting progression in our game.

### Prerequisites

To reach this level, players must have completed the following:

1. Level 3 crafting achievements.
2. Reached a certain in-game level (usually around level 40).

### New Skills

In this stage, players unlock new skills that allow for more complex and advanced crafting. Some examples include:

1. **Masterful Crafting**: This skill increases the quality of items crafted, reducing the chances of defects and increasing the potential for rare and valuable upgrades.
2. **Experimental Recipes**: Players can discover new recipes by experimenting with different materials or combinations. These recipes often lead to more powerful or unique items.
3. **Crafting Speed**: This skill increases the speed at which players craft items, allowing them to create more in less time.
4. **Resource Efficiency**: This skill reduces the amount of resources required to craft items, making production more cost-effective.

### New Items

At this stage, players gain access to a wider variety of materials and can craft higher tier equipment and items. Some examples include:

1. **Legendary Weapons**: These powerful weapons are highly sought after by players due to their increased damage output and unique abilities.
2. **Rare Armor Sets**: These sets offer superior defense and often come with additional bonuses such as increased speed or magical resistance.
3. **Consumables**: Players can craft potions, scrolls, and other consumables that provide temporary boosts to attributes, heal damage, or grant special abilities.

### Achievements

Players can earn new achievements related to crafting in this stage, such as:

1. **Master Crafter**: Earn this achievement by crafting a set of Legendary items.
2. **Alchemist Extraordinaire**: Craft a large number of potions or scrolls to unlock this achievement.
3. **Resourceful**: Save a significant amount of resources while crafting to earn this achievement.
4. **Experimental Genius**: Discover a certain number of new recipes to earn this achievement.
