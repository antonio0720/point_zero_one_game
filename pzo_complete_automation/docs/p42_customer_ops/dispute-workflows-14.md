Dispute Workflows - Version 14
============================

Overview
--------

This document outlines the updated Dispute Workflows version 14 for customer operations, detailing changes and improvements in dispute resolution processes.

Changes and Improvements
-------------------------

### Enhanced Dispute Routing

Dispute routing has been optimized to ensure faster assignment of disputes to relevant teams based on dispute type and priority. This will result in improved response times and resolution efficiency.

### Integration with Fraud Detection System

Version 14 introduces an integration with the company's fraud detection system, allowing for real-time identification and flagging of potentially fraudulent transactions. This will help in early detection and resolution of disputes related to fraud.

### Dispute Escalation Policies

New dispute escalation policies have been implemented to ensure that complex or high-value disputes are handled promptly and efficiently. These policies include escalation triggers, guidelines for escalation, and timelines for resolving escalated disputes.

### Dispute Analytics Dashboard

A new dispute analytics dashboard has been developed to provide insights into dispute trends, team performance, and overall dispute resolution efficiency. This will help in identifying areas for improvement and optimizing the dispute resolution process.

### Improved Communication Channels

Version 14 introduces multiple communication channels for disputes, including email, SMS, and in-app notifications. This will allow for more flexible and efficient communication with customers during the dispute resolution process.

Upgrade Process
---------------

To upgrade to Dispute Workflows version 14, follow these steps:

1. Backup your current dispute workflow settings and data.
2. Download and install the latest version of the customer ops software.
3. Follow the in-app upgrade instructions provided by the system.
4. Configure the new dispute routing rules, fraud detection integration, escalation policies, and communication channels as per your requirements.
5. Test the upgraded system to ensure it is functioning correctly before going live.
