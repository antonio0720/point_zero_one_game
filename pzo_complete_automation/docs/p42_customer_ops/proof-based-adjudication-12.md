# Customer Ops Automation - Proof-Based Adjudication 12

## Overview

This document outlines the Proof-Based Adjudication process for Customer Ops version 12, highlighting its features, benefits, and usage guidelines.

## Features

- Streamlined claims processing with automated decision making based on provided proofs.
- Integration with various data sources for faster and more accurate assessments.
- Customizable rules engine to tailor the adjudication process to specific business needs.
- Real-time notifications and dashboards for easy tracking of claim statuses.
- Compliance with industry standards and regulations.

## Benefits

- Reduced manual intervention, leading to increased efficiency and faster processing times.
- Improved accuracy in claims decisions through automated analysis of provided proofs.
- Enhanced customer experience through quicker resolution of claims.
- Increased operational control and transparency with customizable rules engine and real-time tracking features.
- Improved compliance management due to adherence to industry standards and regulations.

## Usage Guidelines

1. Upload the necessary proofs for each claim in the designated format.
2. The system will automatically analyze the provided proofs and make a decision based on the configured rules.
3. Real-time notifications will be sent regarding the status of each claim.
4. Users can access real-time dashboards to monitor claim progress and manage exceptions if needed.
5. Regularly review and update the rules engine as per business requirements.
