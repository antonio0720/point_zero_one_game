Title: Feature Restrictions - Parental Controls & Age Gating (v12)

## Overview

This document outlines the feature restrictions implemented for Parental Controls and Age Gating in our service (version 12).

## Parental Controls

Parental controls allow parents or guardians to manage their children's access to certain features within the service.

### Feature Restrictions

- Access to private messages: Blocked for users under a specified age.
- Content rating: Restrict access to adult or mature content based on the user's age.
- Communication with strangers: Limit interactions with unknown users.
- Purchase restrictions: Prevent users from making in-app purchases.

## Age Gating

Age gating is a mechanism that verifies the age of users before granting them access to the service.

### Verification Methods

- Date of birth verification: Users must input their date of birth during registration or account setup.
- Account age verification: Accounts created by users under a specified age will be blocked after a certain period, prompting the user to verify their age.

### Age Requirements

The minimum age for using our service is 13 years old, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Local laws and regulations may impose different age requirements.

## Compliance

Our parental controls and age gating mechanisms are designed to comply with relevant data protection laws, including COPPA and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Regular audits and reviews ensure compliance with these regulations.

## User Education

We provide clear and concise information about our parental control features and age gating mechanisms during user registration and account setup. Users are also encouraged to familiarize themselves with these policies on an ongoing basis.

## Enforcement

Violations of our Parental Controls and Age Gating policies may result in account suspension or termination, as well as possible legal action. We reserve the right to monitor user activity for compliance purposes.
