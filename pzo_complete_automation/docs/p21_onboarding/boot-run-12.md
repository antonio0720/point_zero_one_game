# Boot Run 12: Onboarding and Training

## Overview

This document outlines the procedures for the twelfth onboarding and training session (Boot Run 12) at our organization. This program is designed to help new hires and existing employees get up-to-speed with our company's culture, values, and processes.

## Agenda

### Day 1: Welcome and Company Overview

- Introduction to the company
- Mission, vision, and values
- Overview of departments and roles
- Meet the team (executive team, department heads)

### Day 2: Product Training

- Detailed product training sessions
- Hands-on exercises
- Q&A and feedback session

### Day 3: Processes and Tools

- Overview of our processes and workflows
- Introduction to our tools and systems (CRM, project management, etc.)
- Hands-on training with the tools
- Q&A and feedback session

### Day 4: Compliance and Policies

- Overview of company policies and procedures
- Compliance training (data privacy, security, ethics)
- Review of employee handbook

### Day 5: Networking and Social Events

- Team building activities
- Networking sessions with different departments
- Lunch with department heads or executives
- Farewell and next steps discussion

## Expectations

- Active participation in all sessions
- Completion of all assigned tasks and exercises
- Open communication and questions during the sessions
- Positive attitude and willingness to learn
