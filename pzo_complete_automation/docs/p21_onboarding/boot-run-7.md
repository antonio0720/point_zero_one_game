Boot-Run 7: Onboarding and Training
=====================================

Welcome to the seventh step of our onboarding process! In this phase, we will focus on running exercises designed to enhance your training experience.

**Objective:** To familiarize you with the platform's functionalities through hands-on activities.

**Step 1: Accessing the Training Environment**

1. Navigate to the "Training" section in the dashboard.
2. Select the Boot-Run 7 training environment from the list of available options.
3. Click on "Start Session" to initiate the training session.

**Step 2: Exercise Overview**

Once you've accessed the training environment, you will find a list of exercises designed to help you understand different aspects of the platform. Each exercise comes with instructions and objectives that should guide your actions throughout the task.

**Step 3: Completing Exercises**

1. Click on an exercise from the list to begin.
2. Follow the provided instructions and complete the tasks within the exercise.
3. Once you've completed an exercise, click on "Submit" to receive feedback and proceed to the next one.

**Step 4: Evaluating Your Progress**

Your performance in each exercise will be evaluated automatically based on your actions and the objectives of the exercise. The system will provide you with feedback and suggest areas for improvement if necessary.

**Step 5: Seeking Assistance**

If you encounter any issues or have questions during the exercises, feel free to reach out to our support team via chat or email. We're here to help!

**Conclusion**

By completing the Boot-Run 7 exercises, you will develop a strong understanding of the platform and its functionalities. Continue pushing yourself to learn and grow, and remember to take advantage of our support resources whenever needed. Happy training!
