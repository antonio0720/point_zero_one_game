# Mentor Queue - Round 11

This document outlines the details of Mentor Queue round 11, focusing on leaderboards and social aspects.

## Leaderboard

The Mentor Queue leaderboard is a competition that rewards participants for their contributions to the community. The leaderboard ranks users based on their engagement and performance in mentoring others.

### Ranking Criteria

1. **Number of mentees**: Users with more mentees will rank higher, as they have demonstrated a significant impact in helping others.
2. **Active mentorship duration**: Users who maintain active mentorship for extended periods will also rank higher, showcasing their consistency and commitment to the community.
3. **Feedback given**: Providing constructive feedback is crucial for the growth of mentees. Users who offer more quality feedback will see an improvement in their ranking.
4. **Helpful votes**: Receiving helpful votes from the community recognizes a mentor's valuable contributions to the platform, which positively impacts their position on the leaderboard.
5. **Community engagement**: Active participation in discussions and events within the Mentor Queue community can also contribute to a higher ranking on the leaderboard.

### Prizes

Top-ranking users will be rewarded with exclusive prizes, including but not limited to:

1. **Badges of recognition**: Showcasing their achievements and dedication to mentoring others.
2. **Exclusive access to events and workshops**: Providing opportunities for further professional growth and networking.
3. **Mentor Queue merchandise**: Rewarding users with tangible items that symbolize their contributions to the community.
4. **Public recognition**: Acknowledgment of their achievements in Mentor Queue announcements, social media platforms, and other relevant channels.

## Social Aspects

In addition to the competitive leaderboard, Mentor Queue fosters a sense of community among its users through various social features:

1. **Discussion forums**: A platform for users to engage in meaningful conversations about topics related to mentoring and personal development.
2. **Mentor-mentee pairing events**: Regular virtual gatherings that facilitate connections between mentors and mentees, encouraging open communication and shared learning experiences.
3. **Networking opportunities**: Access to a broad network of like-minded individuals who can offer support, advice, and collaboration across various fields and industries.
4. **Community-driven content**: A library of resources created by the Mentor Queue community, including articles, videos, and podcasts that cater to diverse interests and learning styles.
