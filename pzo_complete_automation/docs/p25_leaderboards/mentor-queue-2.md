Mentor Queue 2: Leaderboards & Social Features
==================================================

Mentor Queue 2 introduces new features to enhance the user experience, primarily focusing on leaderboards and social interactions.

Leaderboards
------------

### Overview

The leaderboard system allows users to compete with each other based on various metrics such as the number of solved problems or time taken for solutions. This feature encourages competition and fosters a sense of community among users.

### Leaderboard Types

1. **Global Leaderboard**: A global ranking that includes all users across different regions. Users can see their rankings compared to other participants worldwide.

2. **Regional Leaderboards**: Regional leaderboards are created based on the user's location or self-selected region. This helps users to compete with peers from their area more effectively.

3. **Cohort Leaderboards**: Cohort leaderboards group users into specific timeframes, such as monthly or weekly groups. Users can compare their progress within these cohorts for a more targeted competition.

Social Features
---------------

### Overview

The social features of Mentor Queue 2 aim to create a sense of community and collaboration among users. These new functionalities promote user engagement and make the learning experience more enjoyable.

### Friend System

Users can add friends from within the platform, enabling them to collaborate on solving problems, share tips, and support each other during their learning journey.

### In-app Messaging

The in-app messaging system allows users to communicate directly with their friends or mentors, fostering a more interactive learning environment.

### Notifications

Users receive notifications about new messages, friend requests, and updates on the leaderboards to keep them informed about activities within their network.

By incorporating these features, Mentor Queue 2 aims to create an engaging and competitive learning experience that encourages users to collaborate, compete, and grow together in a supportive community.
