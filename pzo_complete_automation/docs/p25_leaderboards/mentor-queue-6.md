# Mentor Queue 6: Leaderboards and Social Features

This document outlines the features, functionalities, and interactions for Mentor Queue 6, focusing on leaderboards and social elements.

## Overview

Mentor Queue 6 introduces a new level of engagement and competition with its Leaderboard system and enhanced social features. Users can now compare their performance against others, foster collaborative learning, and build a sense of community.

## Leaderboards

### Structure

The leaderboard will be divided into multiple categories based on specific criteria:

1. **Total Points**: Sum of all points earned by a user across various activities (e.g., solving problems, participating in discussions).
2. **Daily Challenges**: Points earned through daily challenges and tasks.
3. **Weekly Challenges**: Points earned from weekly challenges and tasks.
4. **Monthly Challenges**: Points earned through monthly challenges and tasks.
5. **Categories (if applicable)**: Separate leaderboards for specific subject areas, courses, or levels.

### Ranking & Visibility

Each user will have a unique profile page showcasing their rankings in each category, along with relevant statistics such as total points earned and active streaks. Leaderboard positions will be updated in real-time to maintain accuracy and encourage friendly competition.

## Social Features

### User Profiles

Profile pages allow users to showcase their achievements, including a summary of their rankings, badges, and a history of completed challenges. Users can customize their profiles with a profile picture, bio, and other personal details.

### Friends & Challenges

Mentor Queue 6 offers the ability to add friends within the platform. Users can challenge each other to complete specific tasks or problems, fostering friendly competition and promoting collaborative learning.

### In-app Messaging

In-app messaging allows users to communicate with their peers, mentors, and colleagues. This feature enables discussions about challenges, solutions, and strategies for improving performance on the leaderboards.

### Notifications

Users will receive notifications when someone sends a message, accepts a challenge, or reaches a milestone on the leaderboard. Customizable notification settings allow users to control the frequency and types of alerts they receive.

## Conclusion

Mentor Queue 6's Leaderboards and social features aim to create an engaging learning environment that promotes friendly competition, collaboration, and community building among its users. With real-time updates, customizable profiles, in-app messaging, and challenge functions, Mentor Queue 6 is poised to take user engagement to the next level.
