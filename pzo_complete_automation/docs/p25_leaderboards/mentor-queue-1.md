# Mentor Queue 1: Leaderboards and Social Features

This document outlines the key features of the Mentor Queue 1, focusing on its leaderboards and social aspects.

## Overview

The Mentor Queue 1 is a platform designed to facilitate mentorship connections between learners and experts. This section highlights its unique features related to leaderboards and social interactions.

## Leaderboards

The Mentor Queue 1 incorporates a competitive aspect through its leaderboard system. Users can earn points for various activities, such as:

- Successfully completing mentorship sessions
- Giving helpful feedback or advice
- Contributing to the platform's community discussions
- Achieving milestones in their learning journey

The leaderboards will be dynamic, updated regularly to reflect current activity. Users can view and compare their standings with other members.

## Social Interactions

To foster a sense of community and encourage collaboration among users, the Mentor Queue 1 includes various social interaction features:

- User Profiles: Each user has a personal profile page where they can showcase their achievements, skills, and interests.
- In-platform Messaging: Users can send private messages to each other for more focused discussions or mentorship requests.
- Community Forums: A space for users to share ideas, ask questions, and engage in discussions related to the topics covered on the platform.
- Notifications: Users receive notifications for various activities such as new messages, friend requests, and updates to their standings on the leaderboards.

By integrating these features, the Mentor Queue 1 aims to create a dynamic and engaging learning environment where users can connect, learn, and grow together.
