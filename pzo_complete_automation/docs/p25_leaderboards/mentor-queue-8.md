# Mentor Queue 8: Leaderboards & Social Integration

This document outlines the key features, functionality, and integrations for Mentor Queue 8, focusing on its Leaderboards and social components.

## Overview

Mentor Queue 8 is an enhanced version of our mentorship platform, designed to facilitate effective collaboration and knowledge sharing between mentors and mentees. The new features and integrations in this release focus on creating a more engaging and competitive environment through Leaderboards and social connectivity.

## Leaderboards

The Leaderboard feature allows users to compete with one another based on various metrics, such as the number of interactions, completed tasks, or earned badges. This competition aims to encourage active participation, foster a sense of community, and promote continuous learning among our users.

### Leaderboard Types

Mentor Queue 8 offers two types of Leaderboards:

1. **Global Leaderboard**: A list that displays the top performers across all regions and timeframes. This provides a global perspective on user activity and competition.

2. **Regional Leaderboard**: Separate lists for each region, displaying the top performers within that specific area. This allows users to compare their performance with others in their geographical proximity.

### Leaderboard Customization

Administrators can customize the metrics used in the Leaderboards according to their needs and priorities. Examples of available metrics include:

- Number of interactions (messages, meetings, etc.)
- Completed tasks or assignments
- Earned badges or points
- User engagement (time spent on the platform)

## Social Integration

Mentor Queue 8 integrates several social features to enhance user interaction and collaboration:

1. **Profile Pages**: Each user has a personalized profile page where they can showcase their achievements, share their interests, and connect with other users.

2. **Messaging System**: A built-in messaging system allows users to communicate directly with each other, fostering connections and facilitating collaborative learning.

3. **Notifications**: Users receive real-time notifications for important events, such as new messages, task assignments, or updates to their Leaderboard rankings.

4. **Feed/Activity Stream**: A central feed displays the activities of users within the platform, enabling users to stay informed about the latest developments and interact with each other more effectively.

## Conclusion

The introduction of Leaderboards and social integration in Mentor Queue 8 aims to create a more dynamic, engaging, and competitive environment for our users. By fostering collaboration, encouraging active participation, and promoting continuous learning, we hope to enhance the overall user experience and drive greater success within our mentorship community.
