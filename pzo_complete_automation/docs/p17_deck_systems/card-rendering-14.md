# Deck Systems - Card Rendering - Version 14

This document describes the card rendering implementation in version 14 of our deck systems.

## Overview

The card rendering in version 14 has been significantly improved to provide a more visually appealing and user-friendly experience. The changes include:

1. **Enhanced graphics**: New, high-quality graphics have been introduced for all cards, improving their overall appearance.
2. **Animations**: Cards now feature smooth animations during deck shuffling and individual card flips, enhancing the user's experience.
3. **Customizable themes**: Users can now customize the look of their decks by choosing from a variety of pre-built themes or creating their own using CSS.
4. **Responsive design**: The card rendering is optimized for various screen sizes and devices, ensuring a consistent user experience across different platforms.

## Key Features

### Enhanced Graphics

New graphics have been developed for all standard card suits (Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs, Spades) as well as special cards (Jokers, Wild Cards). The graphics are high-resolution and designed to provide a more modern and visually appealing look.

### Animations

Cards now feature smooth animations during deck shuffling and individual card flips. This adds an extra layer of interactivity and makes the overall user experience more engaging.

### Customizable Themes

Users can now customize the look of their decks by choosing from a variety of pre-built themes or creating their own using CSS. This allows users to personalize their deck systems and make them truly unique.

### Responsive Design

The card rendering is optimized for various screen sizes and devices, ensuring a consistent user experience across different platforms. Whether you're playing on a desktop, laptop, tablet, or mobile device, the cards will look great and function smoothly.
