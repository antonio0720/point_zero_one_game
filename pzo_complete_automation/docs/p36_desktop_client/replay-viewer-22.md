## User Interface

The Replay Viewer - Version 22 features a modern and intuitive user interface designed to streamline your replay analysis workflow. Key elements include:

- **Main Menu**: Access all available features and settings through the main menu located at the top of the screen.
- **Replay List**: The replay list on the left sidebar displays all available replays for analysis.
- **Replay Player**: The central area of the application, used to play back recorded game replays.
- **Timeline and Controls**: Located at the bottom of the screen, the timeline and controls allow for precise replay navigation.

## Features

### Replay List

The replay list enables you to browse through all available replays, with options to filter, sort, and search for specific replays based on various criteria such as game version, date, and player.

### Replay Player

The replay player offers a seamless experience when analyzing replays, including frame-by-frame navigation, slow motion playback, and multiple video resolutions to optimize performance.

### Timeline and Controls

The timeline and controls provide you with essential functions for replay analysis, such as:

- **Time Navigation**: Jump to specific points in the replay using the time slider or the seek bar.
- **Playback Speed**: Adjust the playback speed to analyze critical moments at different speeds.
- **Shortcut Keys**: Utilize a variety of shortcut keys for faster navigation and control over the replay player.
