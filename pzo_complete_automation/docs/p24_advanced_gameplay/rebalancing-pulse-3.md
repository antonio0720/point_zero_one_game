Advanced Gameplay - Rebalancing Pulse 3
=======================================

Overview
--------

Rebalancing Pulse 3 is the third major update to our game's balance system, aiming to further refine and optimize the player experience by addressing key areas of imbalance. This document outlines the changes implemented in Rebalancing Pulse 3.

Gameplay Changes
-----------------

### Hero Adjustments

1. **Hero A**
- Base Health decreased by 5%
- Ability X damage increased by 10%
- Ability Y cooldown increased by 2 seconds

2. **Hero B**
- Base Movement Speed increased by 5%
- Ability Z mana cost decreased by 20%
- Ultimate ability duration reduced by 10 seconds

3. **Hero C**
- Base Attack Damage increased by 10%
- Armor increased by 15 points
- Critical Strike chance reduced by 5%

### Item Adjustments

1. **Item A**
- Base Stat X increased by 10%
- Recipe cost increased by 20 gold

2. **Item B**
- Base Stat Y decreased by 10%
- Sell price increased by 50 gold

3. **Item C**
- Unique Passive changed: "Passive - X" to "Passive - Y"
- Build order adjusted to prioritize early game impact

### Map Adjustments

1. **Map A**
- Reduced fog of war visibility in certain areas for improved sightlines
- Added new terrain features to increase strategic depth and combat opportunities

2. **Map B**
- Altered spawn locations for a more balanced start to the game
- Improved pathing and connectivity between key areas

### Game Mode Adjustments

1. **Custom Game Mode X**
- Reduced resource gain rate over time, encouraging quicker pacing and more frequent team fights
- Increased experience gain for kills and assists to promote aggressive playstyles

Conclusion
----------

Rebalancing Pulse 3 aims to create a more engaging and competitive gameplay environment by addressing various aspects of our game. We encourage players to provide feedback on these changes, as we will continue to refine the balance based on community input. Stay tuned for future updates!
