Advanced Gameplay - Rebalancing Pulse 13
=========================================

Overview
--------

Rebalancing Pulse 13 (RP13) is an update aimed at adjusting various aspects of gameplay, focusing on balancing and fine-tuning to provide a more enjoyable and competitive experience for players. This document details the changes made in RP13.

Weapon Adjustments
-------------------

### Assault Rifles

* AK-47: Increased base damage from 26 to 28, but decreased minimum damage from 20 to 19. Reduced max spread after firing the first shot.
* M16A4: Slightly increased base damage from 30 to 31 and reduced recoil.
* M4A1: Decreased recoil slightly and adjusted the vertical recoil pattern for better control.
* SCAR-L: Increased base damage from 28 to 30, but decreased minimum damage from 23 to 22. Reduced max spread after firing the first shot.
* FAL: Slightly increased base damage from 31 to 32 and reduced recoil.

### Submachine Guns

* MP5K: Increased base damage from 23 to 24, but decreased minimum damage from 19 to 18. Reduced max spread after firing the first shot.
* UMP-45: Slightly increased base damage from 26 to 27 and reduced recoil.
* PP-19 Bizon: Decreased recoil slightly and adjusted the vertical recoil pattern for better control.

### Sniper Rifles

* Barrett M82: Increased base damage from 85 to 90, but decreased minimum damage from 70 to 75. Reduced max spread after firing the first shot.
* AWS: Slightly increased base damage from 74 to 76 and reduced recoil.

### Light Machine Guns

* M249: Decreased base damage from 33 to 31 but increased magazine capacity from 100 to 105 rounds. Reduced max spread after firing the first shot.

Grenade Changes
----------------

### Frag Grenade

* Increased explosion radius by 10%.

### Smoke Grenade

* Slightly increased smoke duration and reduced the time it takes for the smoke to fully deploy upon throwing.

Equipment Adjustments
----------------------

### C4

* Reduced explosion radius by 15%.

### Flashbang

* Increased blast radius by 20%.

Vehicle Changes
---------------

### Tank Destroyer

* Slightly increased health from 3,000 to 3,100.

### Attack Helicopter

* Decreased health from 5,400 to 5,200.

Gameplay Changes
----------------

### Breach Charge

* Increased plant time from 5 seconds to 7 seconds.

### Spawn Protection

* Reduced spawn protection timer from 10 seconds to 8 seconds.

### Bomb Sites

* Made minor adjustments to the bomb sites layouts to improve navigation and gameplay flow.

Miscellaneous Changes
----------------------

### Map Rotation

* Added a new map, "Abandoned City," to the active map rotation.
* Removed "Flooded City" from the active map rotation due to balance issues.

### Spectator Camera

* Improved spectator camera functionality, allowing for smoother and more dynamic camera movements during matches.

### Matchmaking Updates

* Implemented a new matchmaking system aimed at reducing queue times and promoting fairer skill-based matchmaking.

This update is intended to improve the overall gameplay experience by balancing various aspects of weapons, equipment, vehicles, and maps. We hope you enjoy the changes and improvements brought by Rebalancing Pulse 13!
