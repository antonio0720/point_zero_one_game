Pulse-8 Rebalancing Changes
=============================

Overview
--------

Pulse-8 is the eighth major balance update for the game, focusing on refining existing mechanics and addressing certain overpowered or underperforming elements. This document details the changes implemented in this update.

Character Balance Changes
--------------------------

### General Adjustments

* Base health of all characters has been increased by 100 points to better accommodate the game's pacing.
* All characters now have a slightly faster base movement speed, promoting more fluid gameplay and faster decision-making.

### Character-specific Changes

#### Aria

* Increased base damage of all basic attacks by 5%.
* Reduced the duration of the `Starlight's Embrace` passive from 3 seconds to 2 seconds.

#### Cadenza

* Increased base damage of all basic attacks by 7%.
* Reduced the cooldown time of the `Grand Pursuit` ability from 10 seconds to 8 seconds.

#### Echo

* Increased base damage of all basic attacks by 5%.
* Increased the range of the `Sonic Boom` ability by 20%.

#### Harmony

* Increased base damage of all basic attacks by 10%.
* Reduced the cooldown time of the `Chord of Destruction` ability from 8 seconds to 6 seconds.

#### Jazz

* Increased base damage of all basic attacks by 7%.
* Increased the duration of the `Jazzy Jive` passive from 3 seconds to 4 seconds.

#### Maestro

* Increased base damage of all basic attacks by 5%.
* Reduced the cooldown time of the `Maestro's Rapture` ability from 10 seconds to 8 seconds.

#### Melody

* Increased base damage of all basic attacks by 7%.
* Increased the duration of the `Melodic Reflection` passive from 2 seconds to 3 seconds.

#### Octave

* Increased base damage of all basic attacks by 5%.
* Reduced the cooldown time of the `Octave's Reverberation` ability from 10 seconds to 8 seconds.

#### Pitch

* Increased base damage of all basic attacks by 7%.
* Reduced the cooldown time of the `Pitch Perfect` ability from 8 seconds to 6 seconds.

#### Rhythm

* Increased base damage of all basic attacks by 5%.
* Reduced the cooldown time of the `Rhythmic Overdrive` ability from 10 seconds to 8 seconds.

#### Scale

* Increased base damage of all basic attacks by 7%.
* Increased the duration of the `Scale's Harmony` passive from 2 seconds to 3 seconds.

#### Tempo

* Increased base damage of all basic attacks by 5%.
* Reduced the cooldown time of the `Tempo's Cadence` ability from 10 seconds to 8 seconds.

### Item Balance Changes

#### Items added:

* `Healing Aura` - Grants a small healing aura around the player, healing nearby allies over time.
* `Power Chord` - Increases damage dealt and reduces cooldown times for all abilities.
* `Rhythmic Resonance` - Enhances the effects of the `Rhythmic Overdrive` ability, increasing its duration and the number of targets affected.

#### Items adjusted:

* `Lullaby` - Reduced base healing per second from 50 to 40.
* `Mystic Chord` - Reduced base cooldown reduction from 15% to 10%.
* `Phantom Melody` - Increased cost from 250 gold to 300 gold.
* `Sonic Boost` - Increased cost from 250 gold to 300 gold.
* `Stardust` - Reduced base damage per second from 75 to 65.
* `Vibrato` - Reduced base cooldown reduction from 15% to 10%.

Map Balance Changes
--------------------

### General Adjustments

* The movement speed bonus granted by the map has been increased slightly, promoting faster gameplay and more fluid movement.

### Map-specific Changes

#### Symphony's Peak

* Reduced the number of shrines from 6 to 5, encouraging players to compete for control of fewer areas.

#### Harmonic Crescendo

* Increased the spawn rate of neutral minions slightly, providing more opportunities for players to farm gold and gain an advantage early in the game.

#### Melodic Battlefield

* Reduced the duration of the `Melodic Battlefield` passive from 6 seconds to 4 seconds, making it easier for teams to regain control of captured points.

#### Rhythmic Arena

* Increased the spawn rate of neutral minions slightly, providing more opportunities for players to farm gold and gain an advantage early in the game.

#### Pitch's Peril

* Reduced the number of shrines from 6 to 5, encouraging players to compete for control of fewer areas.

#### Scale's Sanctuary

* Increased the spawn rate of neutral minions slightly, providing more opportunities for players to farm gold and gain an advantage early in the game.

#### Octave's Overdrive

* Reduced the number of shrines from 6 to 5, encouraging players to compete for control of fewer areas.

#### Aria's Asylum

* Increased the spawn rate of neutral minions slightly, providing more opportunities for players to farm gold and gain an advantage early in the game.

Conclusion
----------

Pulse-8 aims to refine the existing mechanics of the game while addressing certain overpowered or underperforming elements. These changes are designed to create a more balanced and enjoyable experience for all players. We hope you enjoy the updated gameplay!
