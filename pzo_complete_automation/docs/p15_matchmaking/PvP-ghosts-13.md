Title: PvP-ghosts-13 Matchmaking and Sessions

## Overview

The `PvP-ghosts-13` matchmaking system is designed to connect players for competitive gameplay in Player versus Player (PvP) environments. This document outlines the key components of the matchmaking and sessions in the `PvP-ghosts-13` game.

## Matchmaking

The matchmaking process in `PvP-ghosts-13` aims to find suitable opponents for each player based on specific criteria, ensuring a balanced and fair gaming experience. The following factors are considered during the matchmaking process:

1. Player Skill Level: Players are matched with opponents of similar skill levels to ensure competitive gameplay.
2. Region: Players are matched with those located in close geographical proximity to minimize latency issues.
3. Game Preferences: Players can specify their preferred game modes, maps, and number of players per match.
4. Queue Time: The system prioritizes finding matches quickly while still ensuring fair matchups.

## Session Management

Once a match has been found through the matchmaking process, a session is created for the participating players. Each session includes:

1. Game Lobby: A virtual waiting room where players can communicate and prepare for the upcoming match.
2. Match Rules: Specific rules for each game mode are displayed and agreed upon by all participants before the start of the match.
3. In-game Countdown: A countdown timer is presented at the start of each match to ensure all players are ready before it begins.
4. Post-match Statistics: After a session concludes, players receive statistics about their performance in the game, including scores, rankings, and overall progress.

## Player Responsibilities

During a `PvP-ghosts-13` session, players are expected to adhere to certain guidelines to maintain a positive gaming environment:

1. Sportsmanship: Players should exhibit good sportsmanship by respecting their opponents and game officials.
2. Reporting Issues: If any technical or player behavior issues arise during a match, players should report them promptly to the game administrators.
3. Leaving Matches: Leaving a match before its conclusion may result in penalties, including temporary bans or reduced rankings.

By understanding and following these guidelines, players can ensure they have a enjoyable and fair experience when participating in `PvP-ghosts-13` matchmaking sessions.
