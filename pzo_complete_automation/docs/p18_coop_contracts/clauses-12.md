# Co-op Contracts - Clauses 12

## 12.1 Early Termination Clause

The Early Termination Clause outlines the conditions under which either party may terminate the contract before its expiration. This clause typically includes notice periods and penalties for early termination, as well as provisions regarding outstanding obligations upon termination.

```markdown
### 12.1.1 Notice Period

The notice period for termination by either party shall be [specify number of days/weeks]. The notice must be delivered in writing to the other party via registered mail or email with read receipt.

### 12.1.2 Penalties for Early Termination

If the co-op partner elects to terminate this contract prior to its expiration, they shall pay compensation equivalent to [specify amount/percentage] of the remaining contract value as determined by a mutually agreed upon third party.

### 12.1.3 Outstanding Obligations Upon Termination

Upon termination, each party agrees to fulfill all outstanding obligations as outlined in this agreement, including but not limited to:
- Completion of joint projects
- Settlement of financial liabilities
- Return of any assets belonging to the other party
```

## 12.2 Renewal and Extension Clauses

The Renewal and Extension Clauses detail the process for extending or renewing the contract beyond its initial term. This may include automatic renewals, notice periods, and conditions for extension.

```markdown
### 12.2.1 Automatic Renewal

Unless either party provides written notification of non-renewal [specify number of days/weeks] prior to the end of the contract term, this agreement shall automatically renew for another [specify duration] period under the same terms and conditions.

### 12.2.2 Notice Period for Non-Renewal

If either party wishes to not renew this agreement, they must provide written notice [specify number of days/weeks] prior to the end of the contract term. Failure to do so shall be considered an acceptance of automatic renewal.

### 12.2.3 Conditions for Extension

If either party requests an extension to the contract, it must meet the following conditions:
- The request must be made in writing [specify number of days/weeks] prior to the end of the current term.
- Both parties must agree to the proposed terms and conditions of the extension.
```
