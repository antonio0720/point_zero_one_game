# Growth Automation - Abuse Controls - Version 1

## Overview

The `Growth Automation - Abuse Controls - Version 1` document outlines the policies and procedures designed to mitigate potential abuses of the growth automation system. The goal is to maintain a fair, efficient, and secure environment for all users.

## Scope

This document applies to all users who utilize the Growth Automation features within our platform.

## Definitions

1. **Growth Automation**: Refers to automated tools or scripts used to optimize user growth and engagement on our platform.
2. **Abuse**: Unauthorized or malicious use of the Growth Automation system that violates the terms of service, disrupts the platform's normal functioning, or provides an unfair advantage to users.
3. **Terms of Service (ToS)**: The agreement between the user and the platform outlining acceptable usage policies.

## Policy

1. **Prohibited Actions**
- Creating multiple accounts for the purpose of manipulating growth metrics or violating ToS.
- Using scripts or bots to perform actions that are not explicitly allowed within the Growth Automation system.
- Harvesting user data for malicious purposes, such as spamming or phishing.
- Exploiting bugs, vulnerabilities, or loopholes in the Growth Automation system.

2. **Consequences of Abuse**
- Suspension or termination of the offending account(s).
- Account and IP address bans for repeat offenders or severe violations.
- Legal action may be taken for egregious or criminal activities.

3. **Reporting Abuse**
- Users are encouraged to report any suspected abuse of the Growth Automation system by contacting our support team.
- All reports will be investigated, and appropriate action will be taken if abuse is confirmed.

4. **Account Monitoring**
- The platform reserves the right to monitor user accounts for signs of abuse or violations of the ToS.
- Users found to be abusing the system may have their accounts suspended or terminated without warning.
