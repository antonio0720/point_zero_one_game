Deck-Builder-2
==============

Deck-Builder-2 is an internal content authoring tool developed for creating and managing decks in a structured and organized manner. It offers a user-friendly interface with various features that streamline the process of creating presentations, training materials, or any other type of deck.

Features
--------

1. **Drag-and-Drop Interface**: Easily rearrange slides and cards within your decks using the intuitive drag-and-drop interface.

2. **Card Templates**: Customize your content with predefined card templates or create your own for a consistent look across all decks.

3. **Content Types**: Support for various content types such as text, images, videos, and more to ensure versatility in creating engaging decks.

4. **Collaboration**: Collaborate with team members on deck creation and editing in real-time, making it easier to share knowledge and ideas.

5. **Version Control**: Track changes and revert back to any previous version of your decks for easy management and maintenance.

6. **Export Options**: Export your finished decks in various formats like PDF, PowerPoint, HTML, and Markdown, making it simple to share your content with others.

Usage
-----

To use Deck-Builder-2:

1. Log in using your company credentials.
2. Create a new deck or open an existing one.
3. Add cards, images, videos, or any other desired content using the available tools and templates.
4. Arrange the order of the cards by dragging and dropping them within the deck.
5. Collaborate with team members as needed.
6. Track changes and manage versions as you work on your deck.
7. When finished, export your deck in the desired format for easy sharing.

Conclusion
----------

Deck-Builder-2 provides a powerful yet user-friendly tool for creating and managing decks within our organization. Its intuitive interface, versatile features, and collaboration capabilities make it an essential tool for producing high-quality presentations, training materials, and more.
