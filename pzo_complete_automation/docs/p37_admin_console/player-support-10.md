## Overview
The Player Support module allows administrators to manage player queries, issues, and feedback efficiently.

## Accessing Player Support
To access the Player Support module, navigate to the admin console dashboard and click on "Player Support" from the sidebar menu.

## Creating a New Ticket
### Entering Ticket Details
Upon creating a new ticket, fill in the required details such as player username, issue category, subject, and description.

### Attaching Files to the Ticket
You can attach relevant files to the ticket for easier troubleshooting. To do so, click on the "Add Files" button and select the desired files.

## Viewing and Managing Existing Tickets
### Filtering Tickets
Filter tickets by various parameters such as status, player username, or issue category using the search bar and filters available at the top of the ticket list.

### Updating a Ticket
To update an existing ticket, click on the ticket from the list to open it, make changes in the description field, and save your updates.

### Closing a Ticket
Close a ticket when the issue has been resolved by clicking on the "Close" button located at the bottom of the ticket.

## Response Management
### Sending Responses to Players
After reviewing a ticket, send a response to the player using the "Send Response" field. Remember to include any relevant information, steps for resolving the issue, or next actions.

### Managing Multiple Tickets at Once
Select multiple tickets from the list by checking their corresponding boxes and perform bulk actions such as closing or sending responses simultaneously.

## Notification Settings
Customize your notification settings to receive updates on new tickets, ticket responses, and other important events related to Player Support.

## Troubleshooting and FAQs
Find answers to common questions and troubleshoot issues in the dedicated Troubleshooting and FAQs section of the admin console.
