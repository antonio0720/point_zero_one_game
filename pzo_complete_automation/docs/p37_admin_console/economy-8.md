# Economy-8 in Admin Console

## Overview

The Economy-8 module is a feature available within the Admin Console, providing an extensive set of tools and functionalities for managing and optimizing your system's economy.

## Features

1. **Resource Management:** Manage resources such as fuel, steel, and food across your entire empire. Set production rates, monitor consumption, and adjust supply to meet demand effectively.

2. **Trade System:** Facilitate trade between cities within your empire or with external entities. Establish trade agreements, set trade routes, and negotiate prices for resources.

3. **Taxation System:** Implement tax policies to generate revenue for your empire. Adjust tax rates for different resources and cities as needed.

4. **Economic Policies:** Create and manage economic policies that impact various aspects of your empire's economy, such as inflation, employment, and infrastructure development.

5. **Market Analysis:** Analyze market trends, predict future resource demand, and identify opportunities for growth and profit.

6. **Resource Transportation:** Optimize the transportation of resources between cities using efficient routes and modes of transport.

## Usage

To access the Economy-8 module, navigate to the Admin Console dashboard and select "Economy" from the main menu. From there, you can explore each feature in detail and make adjustments as necessary to optimize your empire's economy.
