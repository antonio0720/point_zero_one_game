Content Management (Version 14)
------------------------------

### Overview

### Creating Content

To create new content, navigate to the "Content Management" section in the Admin Console and select "Create Content." Fill out the required fields and click "Save" to publish the new content.

### Editing Content

To edit existing content, locate the desired content item, click on it, make your changes, and then click "Save" to update the content.

### Deleting Content

To delete content, locate the desired content item, click on it, and then click "Delete" in the bottom right corner. Confirm the deletion to permanently remove the content.

### Managing Categories

Categories help organize your content. To create a new category, navigate to "Content Management" > "Categories" > "Create Category." To edit or delete an existing category, click on the desired category and make your changes before clicking "Save."

### Organizing Content with Tags

Tags allow you to group related content together. To add tags to a content item, navigate to the "Tags" section when creating or editing content and enter the desired tags separated by commas.

### Publishing and Unpublishing Content

By default, all newly created content is unpublished. You can publish content by navigating to the content item and clicking on "Publish." To unpublish content, click on "Unpublish."

### Scheduling Content

To schedule content for publication at a specific date and time, navigate to the content item and set the desired date and time in the "Published At" field. The content will be automatically published at the specified time.

### Version Control

Version control allows you to track changes made to your content over time. To view previous versions of a content item, click on the desired content item and select "View Previous Versions."

### User Permissions

User permissions determine which actions users can perform in Content Management. To manage user permissions, navigate to "Admin Console" > "Users" > "[Username]" > "Content Management Permissions." Set the desired permissions for each user accordingly.
