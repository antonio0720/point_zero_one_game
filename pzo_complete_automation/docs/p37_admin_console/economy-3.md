Economy-3 in the Admin Console
===============================

Economy-3 is a comprehensive feature within the Admin Console that provides powerful tools for managing and monitoring your game's economy. This documentation outlines the key features of Economy-3.

**Currency Management**
-----------------------

### Currencies

Manage multiple currencies, each with unique names, icons, and denominations. You can also set exchange rates between different currencies.

### Balances

View and manage the balances of individual players or entire groups. Adjust balances as needed to ensure a fair and balanced game environment.

**Economy Events**
-------------------

### Event Types

Define various types of economy events, such as daily rewards, weekly challenges, or special promotions. Customize event details, including start and end times, and reward structures.

### Event Scheduling

Schedule events to run automatically at specified times or on specific days. You can also set recurring events for a continuous stream of content.

**In-Game Shops**
------------------

### Shop Items

Create and manage a diverse selection of shop items, such as resources, consumables, and cosmetic enhancements. Each item can have unique names, descriptions, images, and prices in various currencies.

### Shop Categories

Organize your shop items into categories for easy navigation and browsing by players. You can also set shop-wide sale discounts or limit the availability of specific items during certain events.

**Monetization**
----------------

### In-App Purchases

Offer premium content through in-app purchases, allowing players to purchase currency packages, exclusive items, or other upgrades using real money. You can set different pricing tiers for each item based on currency and region.

### Premium Currency

Manage a premium currency that can be earned through gameplay or purchased with real money. This currency can be used exclusively to acquire high-value items or exclusive content.

**Analytics & Reporting**
--------------------------

### Sales Tracking

Monitor sales data for in-app purchases and currency packages, including total revenue, top-selling items, and player spending habits. Use this information to optimize your monetization strategy and identify areas for improvement.

### Player Behavior Analysis

Analyze player behavior patterns related to economy interactions, such as purchasing frequency, favorite items, and most active hours. This data can help you tailor content and promotions to better engage players and drive revenue.
