LiveOps Control Plane - Rollout Dashboards (v10)
==============================================

Overview
--------

The LiveOps Control Plane Rollout Dashboards provide a comprehensive view of the rollouts and their progress in your organization. This document outlines the features, functionalities, and usage of Rollout Dashboards version 10.

Key Features
------------

1. **Overview Dashboard**: Displays a high-level summary of all active rollouts across your organization, including rollout status, progress percentage, and time since initiation.

2. **Rollout Detail View**: Offers an in-depth look at each individual rollout, showing the rollout configuration, traffic distribution, key metrics, and health of the rollout.

3. **Progress Graphs**: Visual representation of rollout progress over time, including total users and traffic affected, successes, failures, and active user count.

4. **Rollout Health Overview**: Displays the overall health of all active rollouts in a single view, using color-coded indicators for each rollout's status (healthy, warning, critical).

5. **Filtering and Sorting**: Allows users to filter and sort rollouts based on various criteria such as environment, application, and rollout type, making it easier to focus on specific rollouts.

6. **Rollout Events Log**: Provides a detailed log of events associated with each rollout, including the creation, updates, and status changes, helping users stay informed about the progress and status of their rollouts.

Usage Guide
-----------

1. Access the Rollout Dashboards by navigating to the LiveOps Control Plane web interface.
2. The Overview Dashboard will be displayed by default, showing a summary of all active rollouts.
3. To view detailed information about a specific rollout, click on its row in the Overview Dashboard or use the search bar to find it quickly.
4. In the Rollout Detail View, you can examine the configuration, metrics, and health of the rollout, as well as monitor the progress graphs and events log.
5. Use the filters at the top of the screen to narrow down the list of rollouts based on your requirements.
6. To go back to the Overview Dashboard, click the "Overview" button in the top navigation bar.

Tips and Best Practices
------------------------

1. Regularly monitor the Rollout Health Overview to identify any potential issues that require attention.
2. Use filters to focus on rollouts related to specific applications or environments for a more targeted analysis.
3. Keep an eye on the progress graphs to understand how your rollouts are performing over time and make data-driven decisions about scaling or pausing them.
4. Review the events log frequently to stay informed about the status of your rollouts and quickly respond to any issues that may arise.
5. Leverage LiveOps Control Plane's integration with your monitoring tools to receive alerts when rollout health or key metrics deviate from expected values.
