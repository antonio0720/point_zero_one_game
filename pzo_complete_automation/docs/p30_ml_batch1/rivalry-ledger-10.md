Rivalry Ledger 10 (ML Batch 1)
===============================

Rivalry Ledger 10 is the tenth release in the series of Rivalry Ledgers, specifically designed for Machine Learning (ML) Batch 1. This ledger introduces a new set of rules and regulations to ensure fairness, transparency, and efficiency in ML competitions hosted by Rivalry.

Competition Structure
----------------------

Rivalry Ledger 10 features a series of ML competitions, each with its own specific task and dataset. Participants are expected to develop predictive models using their preferred ML algorithms and submit their solutions for evaluation.

Scoring System
---------------

The scoring system in Rivalry Ledger 10 is designed to measure the performance of submitted models based on a set of predefined metrics. The model with the highest score will be ranked at the top of the leaderboard.

Data Availability
------------------

All necessary data for each competition will be made available through the Rivalry platform before the start of the corresponding event. Participants are expected to use this data exclusively in their models.

Submission Format
-----------------

Participants should submit their solutions in a specified format, usually a CSV or JSON file containing predictions for the test dataset. Submissions must adhere to the guidelines provided for each competition.

Leaderboard and Rankings
-------------------------

The Rivalry leaderboard displays the rankings of all participants based on their scores. The rankings will be updated in real-time during the competition, allowing participants to monitor their progress.

Prize Pool Distribution
------------------------

Rivalry Ledger 10 offers a significant prize pool for winners of each competition. The distribution of the prize money will be determined based on the final rankings and the specific rules for each event.

Rules and Regulations
----------------------

Participants are expected to read and adhere to the rules and regulations provided for each competition. Violations may result in disqualification or other penalties, as outlined in the rules.
