# Asset Systems - Portfolio Heat-10

## Overview

The Portfolio Heat-10 is a comprehensive asset system designed to manage and analyze a diverse portfolio of up to 10 assets. It offers features for tracking asset performance, generating reports, and making informed investment decisions.

## Features

### Asset Tracking

The system allows users to add and remove assets from their portfolio, with real-time updates on asset price, market capitalization, and other crucial metrics.

### Performance Analysis

Portfolio Heat-10 provides detailed performance analysis for each asset, including returns, risk ratios, and growth trends over time.

### Portfolio Diversity Score

The system calculates a diversification score based on the portfolio's asset distribution to help users understand their investment risks.

### Customizable Alerts

Users can set up custom alerts for specific assets or portfolio-wide events, ensuring they stay informed about market movements.

### Report Generation

Portfolio Heat-10 offers a variety of report templates, including performance summaries, asset allocation breakdowns, and risk analysis reports.

## Getting Started

To start using Portfolio Heat-10, follow these steps:

1. Register for an account on the [Portfolio Heat-10 website](https://www.portfolioheat-10.com).
2. Verify your email and set up two-factor authentication for added security.
3. Link your exchange accounts to import assets into your portfolio.
4. Customize your alerts settings and preferred report templates.
5. Begin tracking your investments and receiving real-time updates on your portfolio's performance.
