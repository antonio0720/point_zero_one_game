Battle Pass Season 9
====================

Welcome to Battle Pass Season 9! Embark on an exciting journey filled with new challenges, rewards, and experiences. Here's a rundown of the achievements, quests, and what you can expect from our ninth Battle Pass.

**Battle Pass Purchase and Levels**
------------------------------------

1. Acquire the Battle Pass for 900 Crystals or Battle Coins to unlock access to the Season 9 rewards track.
2. Progress through 75 levels, earning exclusive rewards along the way.

**Free Track Rewards**
-----------------------

### Level 1 - Unlock
- Common Emote: Excited!
- Common Spray: Battle Pass 9

### Levels 2 - 40
- Variety of Common, Rare, and Epic cosmetic items including Emotes, Sprays, and Loading Screens.

### Level 41 - Season 8 Exclusive
- Legendary Skin: Season 8 Champion

**Battle Pass Track Rewards**
-----------------------------

### Level 1 (Purchased) - Unlock
- Epic Emote: Dance Party
- Epic Spray: Battle Pass 9 Tag

### Levels 2 - 75 (Purchased)
- Variety of Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary cosmetic items including Emotes, Sprays, Loading Screens, Skins, and Victory Poses.

**Bonus Rewards**
------------------

### Reaching Level 50 (Purchased)
- Epic Skin: Tempest's Fury (Exclusive to Battle Pass owners)

### Reaching Level 75 (Purchased)
- Legendary Skin: The Chronomancer (Exclusive to Battle Pass owners)

**Quests and Achievements**
----------------------------

Throughout the Season, take on various quests and achievements to earn additional bonuses and unlock extra rewards.

### Daily Quests
1. Win a match (3/day) - Reward: 500 XP
2. Deal damage with a primary weapon (1000/day) - Reward: 500 XP
3. Get kills with a secondary weapon (5/day) - Reward: 500 XP
4. Get kills with an ultimate ability (2/day) - Reward: 500 XP

### Weekly Quests
1. Win 10 matches in any game mode - Reward: 3000 XP and a random cosmetic item
2. Deal a total of 50,000 damage with primary weapons - Reward: 3000 XP and a random cosmetic item
3. Get 100 kills with a secondary weapon - Reward: 3000 XP and a random cosmetic item
4. Get 20 kills with an ultimate ability - Reward: 3000 XP and a random cosmetic item
5. Win 5 matches in Arena mode - Reward: 3000 XP and a random cosmetic item
6. Win 5 matches in Battle Royale mode - Reward: 3000 XP and a random cosmetic item
7. Win 5 matches in Siege mode - Reward: 3000 XP and a random cosmetic item

### Achievements
1. Complete the Battle Pass Track (Purchased) - Reward: A title and a unique spray
2. Complete the Free Track (Non-Purchased) - Reward: A title and a unique spray
3. Win 50 matches in any game mode - Reward: A title and a unique spray
4. Deal 1,000,000 damage with primary weapons - Reward: A title and a unique spray
5. Get 2,000 kills with secondary weapons - Reward: A title and a unique spray
6. Get 500 kills with ultimate abilities - Reward: A title and a unique spray
7. Win 100 matches in Arena mode - Reward: A title and a unique spray
8. Win 100 matches in Battle Royale mode - Reward: A title and a unique spray
9. Win 100 matches in Siege mode - Reward: A title and a unique spray
