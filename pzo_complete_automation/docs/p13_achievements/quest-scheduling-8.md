Title: Quest Scheduling (v8)

## Overview

The Quest Scheduling system (version 8) is an integral part of the game's progression and rewards system, combining Achievements, Quests, and Battle Pass. This document details the key components and updates in the v8 release.

## Components

### Achievements

Achievements are long-term goals that players can strive for, often providing significant rewards upon completion. They vary in difficulty, from easy achievements to those requiring dedicated effort and time investment.

### Quests

Quests are short-term objectives given to players to complete within a certain period. Completing quests earns players experience points, items, and other rewards. There are Daily Quests, Weekly Quests, and Special Event Quests available in the game.

### Battle Pass

The Battle Pass is a premium feature offering exclusive rewards as players progress through its tiers by earning Battle Points (BP). It features seasonal content and limited-time challenges to keep the experience fresh and engaging.

## Quest Scheduling System v8 Updates

### New Daily & Weekly Quests

New Daily and Weekly Quests have been added to provide more variety and engage players daily. These quests include various objectives like defeating specific enemies, completing activities within a certain timeframe, or accomplishing tasks related to Achievements.

### Improved Quest Rewards

Quest rewards have been revised to offer better incentives for players. Completing Daily and Weekly Quests now provides more experience points, in-game currency, and exclusive cosmetic items.

### Battle Pass Updates

The Battle Pass system has received several enhancements, including:

1. Increased tiers for more rewards.
2. New themed seasons with unique rewards.
3. Limited-time challenges to earn additional Battle Points (BP).
4. Improved user interface for easier navigation and tracking of progress.

### Achievement Updates

A few adjustments have been made to the Achievement system:

1. New Achievements have been added, focusing on various aspects of gameplay.
2. Difficulty levels of some existing Achievements have been adjusted to provide a more balanced challenge for players.
3. Improved user interface and tracking system for Achievements.

### Optimizations & Bug Fixes

Various optimizations and bug fixes have been implemented across the entire Quest Scheduling system to ensure smooth gameplay and minimize issues that may affect players' progression and rewards.
