# Seasonal Rewards 10

This document outlines the rewards for the tenth season of Achievements, Quests, and Battle Pass in our game.

## Achievement Rewards

### Bronze Tier

- Uncommon Token x3
- Common Token x5

### Silver Tier

- Rare Token x4
- Uncommon Token x6
- Common Token x8

### Gold Tier

- Epic Token x5
- Rare Token x7
- Uncommon Token x10
- Common Token x12

## Quest Rewards

### Easy Quests

- Completion: 10 Common Tokens
- Daily: 3 Common Tokens

### Medium Quests

- Completion: 20 Rare Tokens
- Daily: 5 Rare Tokens

### Hard Quests

- Completion: 40 Epic Tokens
- Daily: 10 Epic Tokens

## Battle Pass Rewards

### Free Tier (Level 1 - 10)

- Common Token x2 per level
- Uncommon Item x1 at Level 5 and Level 10

### Premium Tier (Level 11 - 30)

- Rare Token x2 per level
- Epic Item x1 at Level 15, Level 20, and Level 30
