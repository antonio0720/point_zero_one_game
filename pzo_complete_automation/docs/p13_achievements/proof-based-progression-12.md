# Proof-Based Progression - Level 12

This document outlines the achievements, quests, and battle pass for level 12 in the Proof-Based Progression system.

## Achievements

### Mathematical Mastery
#### Proving Grounds: Calculus I
- **Description:** Solve 10 complex calculus problems using the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus.
- **Reward:** 250 XP, +3% damage bonus in PvE battles.

#### Proving Grounds: Limits and Series
- **Description:** Successfully evaluate 10 limit and series problems involving infinite sequences or summations.
- **Reward:** 300 XP, +4% damage resistance in PvP battles.

### Logical Leaps
#### Propositional Reasoning
- **Description:** Complete a set of 8 logic puzzles using truth tables and the rules of propositional logic.
- **Reward:** 200 XP, +2% resource gathering speed.

#### Set Theory Exploration
- **Description:** Solve 10 problems involving set operations such as union, intersection, and complement.
- **Reward:** 350 XP, +5% resource storage capacity.

## Quests

### The Calculus Quest
1. **Introduction to Rates of Change:** Solve 3 basic calculus problems related to derivatives and rates of change (100 XP)
2. **Antiderivatives and Definite Integrals:** Solve 5 problems involving antiderivatives and definite integrals (200 XP)
3. **Calculus Applications:** Solve 2 real-world problems using calculus concepts (300 XP)
4. **Advanced Calculus Problems:** Solve 3 complex calculus problems requiring the use of multiple techniques (400 XP)
5. **The Final Derivative:** Solve a challenging derivate problem to claim the bonus rewards (500 XP, +2% damage resistance in PvE battles)

### The Logic Quest
1. **Introduction to Propositional Logic:** Complete 3 basic logic puzzles using truth tables (100 XP)
2. **Logical Deductions:** Solve 5 logic problems using rules of propositional logic (200 XP)
3. **Logical Fallacies:** Identify and explain the reasoning behind 4 common logical fallacies (300 XP)
4. **Advanced Logic Puzzles:** Solve 3 complex logic puzzles requiring advanced techniques (400 XP)
5. **The Final Deduction:** Solve a challenging logic puzzle to claim the bonus rewards (500 XP, +2% resource gathering speed)

## Battle Pass

### Level 1-10 Rewards:
- Tier 1: Common Resource Bundle (20 each of common resources - wood, stone, and ore)
- Tier 2: Rare Resource Bundle (20 each of rare resources - advanced alloy, crystalline shard, and exotic dust)
- Tier 3: Epic Resource Bundle (15 each of epic resources - enchanted essence, mythril ore, and radiant crystal)
- Tier 4: Legendary Resource Bundle (10 each of legendary resources - astral gemstone, divine shard, and celestial crystal)
- Tier 5: Exclusive Cosmetic Item (a unique hat or cape)

### Level 11-20 Rewards:
- Tier 6: Epic Weapon Skin (a visually striking weapon appearance)
- Tier 7: Legendary Armor Set (an impressive set of armor with enhanced stats)
- Tier 8: Mythical Mount (a powerful mount with increased speed and unique abilities)
- Tier 9: Epic Title (a prestigious title to display your achievements)
- Tier 10: Legendary Title (a rare and highly coveted title for the most dedicated players)
