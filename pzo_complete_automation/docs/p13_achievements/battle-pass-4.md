Battle Pass 4
=============

Welcome to Battle Pass 4! This is your guide for all the exciting achievements, quests, and rewards that await you in this season. Let's dive in!

**Battle Pass Progression**
----------------------------

Progress through the battle pass by earning XP from matches, leveling up, and completing daily and weekly challenges. Each level offers a unique reward, ranging from cosmetic items to exclusive emotes.

### Free Battle Pass Rewards

1. Tier 1 - Starter Pack (Weapon Skin, Emote)
2. Tier 4 - Uncommon Weapon Skin (Weapon Skin)
3. Tier 7 - Rare Emote (Emote)
4. Tier 10 - Epic Weapon Skin (Weapon Skin)
5. Tier 13 - Legendary Emote (Emote)
6. Tier 17 - Mythic Weapon Skin (Weapon Skin)

### Battle Pass Rewards (Premium)

By purchasing the premium battle pass, you'll have access to even more rewards:

1. Tier 1 - Starter Pack (Weapon Skin, Emote) + Battle Bundle (Epic Weapon Skin, Epic Emote)
2. Tier 25 - Mythic Weapon Skin (Weapon Skin)
3. Tier 30 - Exclusive Legendary Emote (Emote)

**Daily Challenges**
---------------------

Complete a set of daily challenges each day to earn additional XP and rewards. These challenges refresh daily, so make sure you check back often!

Examples of Daily Challenges:
- Win 3 Matches
- Deal 500 Damage with Sniper Rifles
- Use Healing Items 10 times

**Weekly Challenges**
----------------------

Complete a new set of weekly challenges each week to earn even more XP and rewards. These challenges reset every seven days, so don't miss out!

Examples of Weekly Challenges:
- Win 10 Matches with a Sniper Rifle
- Deal 2,500 Damage to Enemy Players as a Team (Team Challenge)
- Revive Teammates 3 times in a single match

**Achievements**
------------------

Earn extra XP and rewards by completing various achievements throughout the season. These challenges range from gameplay feats, to achieving high scores, to collecting specific items. Some achievements are one-time challenges, while others can be completed multiple times.

Examples of Achievements:
- Headshot Master: Get 100 headshots in a single match (Reward: Epic Weapon Skin)
- Survivalist: Survive for 5 minutes without taking any damage (Reward: Rare Emote)
- Collector: Collect all the hidden items in a map (Reward: Mythic Emote)
