# Monetization Rules for PZO Sovereign Infrastructure Architect - Financial Roguelike Game

This document outlines the allowed and forbidden monetization practices within the Point Zero One Digital (PZO) game, ensuring a fair and transparent gaming experience.

## Allowed Monetization Practices

1. **Cosmetics**: Players can purchase cosmetic items such as skins, emotes, or avatars that do not provide any competitive advantage in the game.

2. **Creator Tools**: Advanced tools and resources for creating custom content within the game may be offered for purchase by developers and power users.

3. **Priority Verification Speed**: Players can opt to pay for faster verification speeds, ensuring quicker access to new game updates or events. However, this does not provide any in-game advantages.

## Forbidden List

The following practices are strictly prohibited within the PZO game:

1. **Tiers/Ladders**: Any system that grants players access to exclusive content, features, or gameplay based on their financial investment is forbidden.

2. **Stamps**: Stamp systems that reward players with unique items or advantages based on their in-game achievements are not allowed.

3. **Invites**: Invite systems that offer incentives such as bonus resources or exclusive content to players who invite friends to the game are prohibited.

## Trust Rule

To maintain a fair and trustworthy gaming environment, all transactions within the PZO game will be transparent and verifiable. Players can trust that their progress and purchases are secure and that no unauthorized modifications will occur.
