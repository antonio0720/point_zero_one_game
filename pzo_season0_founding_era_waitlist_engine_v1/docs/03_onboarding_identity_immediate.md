## Overview

This onboarding process is designed to get you up and running in just 60 seconds. We prioritize a guest-first approach, ensuring a seamless experience with no shame or pressure. The single-next-action UX principle guides the flow, making it clear what your first step should be.

## Non-Negotiables

1. **Execution-Grade Language**: Precise and concise language is used throughout to ensure clarity and efficiency. Zero fluff, anti-bureaucratic.
2. **'No Shame' Copy Rules**: We believe in creating a welcoming environment for all users. Our copy is designed to be inclusive and non-judgmental.
3. **Retention Rationale**: Every decision in this onboarding process is made with the goal of keeping you engaged and coming back for more.
4. **Guest-First Path**: We prioritize the needs and experience of our guests, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable first interaction.
5. **Single-Next-Action UX Principle**: The user interface guides you through the process by clearly indicating the next action to take at all times.

## Implementation Spec

1. **Step 1: Introduction**
   - Greet the user warmly and briefly explain who we are and what we offer.
   - Provide a clear call-to-action (CTA) for the next step.

2. **Step 2: Account Creation**
   - Offer a simple, quick account creation process with minimal required information.
   - Emphasize the benefits of creating an account and the ease of the process.

3. **Step 3: Game Introduction**
   - Briefly introduce the game, its unique features, and why it's worth playing.
   - Provide a CTA to start playing immediately.

4. **Step 4: Tutorial Overview**
   - Explain the tutorial process in simple terms, emphasizing its purpose and benefits.
   - Offer an opt-out for experienced users who prefer to dive right in.

5. **Step 5: Tutorial Start**
   - Begin the tutorial if the user has chosen to proceed.
   - Continue to guide the user through the game with clear, concise instructions.

6. **Step 6: Post-Tutorial**
   - Congratulate the user on completing the tutorial.
   - Offer suggestions for further engagement, such as joining a community or exploring additional features.

## Edge Cases

1. **User Opted Out of Tutorial**: If the user chooses to skip the tutorial, provide immediate access to the game and offer optional tips or guides for new players.
2. **Account Creation Failure**: If account creation fails due to an error, offer a clear explanation and suggest alternative solutions, such as resetting the password or contacting support.
