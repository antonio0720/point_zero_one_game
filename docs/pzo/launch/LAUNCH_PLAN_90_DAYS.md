# 90-Day Launch Plan
=====================

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Days 1-30)
---------------------------

### Deliverables:

*   **Documentation**: Complete documentation of the product and its features.
*   **Content Calendar**: A content calendar outlining the launch plan, including blog posts, social media, and email marketing campaigns.
*   **Marketing Materials**: High-quality marketing materials, including images, videos, and press releases.

### Content Cadence:

*   Week 1-2: Product documentation and content calendar development
*   Week 3-4: Marketing material creation and review
*   Week 5-6: Finalize pre-launch preparations

## Phase 2: Launch (Days 31-60)
-------------------------

### Deliverables:

*   **Product Launch**: Successful product launch, including deployment to production environment.
*   **Launch Announcement**: Public announcement of the product launch, including press releases and social media posts.
*   **Customer Onboarding**: Complete customer onboarding process, including setup and configuration.

### Content Cadence:

*   Week 7-8: Finalize product preparation for launch
*   Week 9-10: Launch announcement and marketing campaigns
*   Week 11-12: Customer onboarding and support

## Phase 3: Post-Launch (Days 61-90)
---------------------------

### Deliverables:

*   **Customer Support**: Ongoing customer support, including issue resolution and feedback collection.
*   **Product Feedback**: Collection and implementation of product feedback from customers.
*   **Marketing Analysis**: Analysis of marketing campaigns and launch success metrics.

### Content Cadence:

*   Week 13-14: Customer support and feedback collection
*   Week 15-16: Product development based on customer feedback
*   Week 17-18: Marketing analysis and campaign optimization
