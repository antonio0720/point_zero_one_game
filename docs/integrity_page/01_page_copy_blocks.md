# Point Zero One Digital - Sovereign Infrastructure Architecture

A 12-minute financial roguelike game, built with production-grade, deployment-ready code.

## Hero

The player is a sovereign entity navigating the financial landscape, making strategic decisions to maximize wealth and influence.

### Non-negotiables

- Strict TypeScript adherence: Never use 'any'. All code is strict-mode.
- Deterministic effects: Every action results in predictable outcomes.

## Pillars

1. **Hero**: The player character, with unique attributes and abilities.
2. **Financial Market Simulation**: Realistic financial market dynamics for a challenging experience.
3. **Decision-making Mechanics**: Strategic choices that impact the hero's wealth and influence.
4. **Progression System**: Leveling up, unlocking new abilities, and acquiring resources.
5. **Random Events**: Unpredictable events that test the player's strategic skills.

## Lifecycle

1. **Onboarding**: Introduce players to the game mechanics and financial market.
2. **Gameplay Loop**: 12-minute sessions of strategic decision-making and market simulation.
3. **Endgame**: Achieve maximum wealth and influence, or face bankruptcy.
4. **Post-game**: Analyze performance, compare with others, and prepare for the next session.

## Proof Anatomy

Proof is a digital asset that represents the player's wealth and influence. It can be traded, used to unlock new abilities, or lost in strategic decisions.

### Non-negotiables

- Proofs are unique and non-fungible.
- Proofs have intrinsic value based on their rarity and the player's influence.

## Ladder Rules

The ladder ranks players based on their wealth and influence. Higher rankings offer benefits, such as access to exclusive events or resources.

### Non-negotiables

- Rankings are updated at the end of each game session.
- Players can challenge others for positions on the ladder.

## Device Trust Phrasing

The game requires trust in the device running it to ensure fairness and security.

### Non-negotiables

- The device must be trusted before it can be used to play the game.
- Trust is established through a secure, verifiable process.

## Quarantine/Appeals

Suspicious activity may result in a player's account being quarantined. Players can appeal the decision and provide evidence of their innocence.

### Non-negotiables

- All decisions are made by an impartial review board.
- The review process is transparent and fair.

## Transparency Report

Regular reports detailing game mechanics, financial transactions, and player data privacy will be published.

### Non-negotiables

- Reports will be clear, concise, and easy to understand.
- Reports will be made available to all players.

## Technical Appendix

A comprehensive technical document detailing the game's architecture, codebase, and deployment process.

### Non-negotiables

- The document will be written in a clear, execution-grade language.
- The document will include edge cases and implementation specifics.
