# Weekly LiveOps Calendar Template

This template outlines the schedule for weekly live operations in Point Zero One Digital's financial roguelike game.

## Overview

The calendar consists of events scheduled on Monday, Wednesday, Friday, and Sunday. Each day serves a specific purpose to engage players and maintain a dynamic game environment.

## Non-Negotiables

1. **Monday**: Fresh card drop and weekly challenge announcement.
2. **Wednesday**: Mid-week engagement push.
3. **Friday**: Founder Night ladder opens.
4. **Sunday**: Week close, snapshot, and Verified Proof of the Week highlight.

## Implementation Spec

### Monday

- Fresh card drop: Distribute new cards to players, ensuring a balanced and challenging gameplay experience.
- Weekly challenge announcement: Introduce a new challenge with clear objectives, rewards, and deadlines.

### Wednesday

- Mid-week engagement push: Implement in-game events or promotions to encourage player interaction and retention.

### Friday

- Founder Night ladder opens: Allow access to exclusive game modes or rewards for early adopters of the game.

### Sunday

- Week close: End ongoing challenges, prepare for new ones, and perform necessary maintenance tasks.
- Snapshot: Take a backup of the game state for future analysis and potential rollbacks in case of issues.
- Verified Proof of the Week highlight: Showcase notable player achievements or strategies to foster a competitive spirit among players.

## Edge Cases

In case of unforeseen circumstances (e.g., server downtime, critical bugs), adjustments may be necessary to ensure smooth game operations. Communicate any changes to players promptly and transparently.
