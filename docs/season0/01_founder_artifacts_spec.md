# Founder Artifacts Specification

## Overview

This document outlines the specifications for Founder Titles, Founder Cosmetics, Proof-Card Stamps, permanence rules, upgrade paths, and visibility surfaces in Point Zero One Digital's 12-minute financial roguelike game.

## Non-negotiables

1. **Founder Titles**: Unique titles for early supporters of the game, serving as a testament to their commitment and support.
2. **Founder Cosmetics**: Exclusive in-game cosmetic items for early supporters, enhancing their character's appearance.
3. **Proof-Card Stamps**: Distinctive visual elements on Proof-Cards, signifying the holder's status as a Founder.
4. **Permanence Rules**: Founder Titles, Cosmetics, and Proof-Card Stamps are permanent and non-transferable within the game.
5. **Upgrade Paths**: No upgrade paths for Founder items; they remain at their initial state.
6. **Visibility Surfaces**: Founder items are visible to all players in the game, promoting transparency and recognition of early supporters.

## Implementation Spec

### Founder Titles

- Titles will be displayed above the player's character name in the game.
- Titles will be unique and descriptive, reflecting the level of support.

### Founder Cosmetics

- Cosmetic items will include exclusive skins for characters, weapons, and other in-game assets.
- These items will not provide any gameplay advantages but will enhance visual appeal.

### Proof-Card Stamps

- Proof-Cards are digital collectibles that serve as proof of ownership for certain in-game items or achievements.
- The Founder stamp will be a distinctive visual element on each Proof-Card, signifying the holder's status.

## Edge Cases

1. **Account Deletion**: If a player deletes their account, they forfeit all Founder items associated with that account. No exceptions.
2. **Game Updates**: Founder items will remain unchanged during game updates, unless explicitly stated otherwise in the update's release notes.
3. **Bug Fixes**: In case of bugs affecting Founder items, priority will be given to fixing the issue while maintaining the permanence and integrity of the items.
