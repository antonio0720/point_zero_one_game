This phase spans from Day 61 to Day 90, following the end of the campaign. The primary focus during this period is on expanding distribution channels and activating key initiatives.

## Non-negotiables

1. **DTC Store Opening**: Simultaneously with the campaign's conclusion, our Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) store must be launched to ensure immediate sales opportunities.
2. **Amazon + Retail Pitch Deck**: Prepare a comprehensive pitch deck for Amazon and retail partnerships, detailing game features, market potential, and revenue projections.
3. **Bulk Partnership Outreach**: Initiate targeted outreach efforts towards employers, churches, schools, and other relevant organizations to establish bulk purchase agreements.
4. **Play-to-Prosper Pledge Activation**: Launch the Play-to-Prosper Pledge, encouraging players to support the game's development through donations or sponsorships.

## Implementation Spec

1. **DTC Store Preparation**: Ensure that the store is fully stocked with game copies and ready for immediate sales upon campaign conclusion.
2. **Pitch Deck Creation**: Collaborate with marketing and finance teams to create a compelling pitch deck, focusing on data-driven insights and clear revenue projections.
3. **Outreach Strategy Development**: Develop an outreach strategy that targets key organizations, including email templates, social media messaging, and potential partnership incentives.
4. **Play-to-Prosper Pledge Launch**: Design a user-friendly interface for the Play-to-Prosper Pledge, making it easy for players to contribute to the game's development.

## Edge Cases

1. **DTC Store Scalability**: Monitor store performance closely and scale up inventory as needed to accommodate increased demand.
2. **Partnership Negotiations**: Anticipate potential roadblocks during partnership negotiations, such as contract disputes or conflicting interests, and have contingency plans in place.
3. **Pledge Contribution Management**: Implement a secure system for managing pledge contributions, ensuring transparency and accountability.
