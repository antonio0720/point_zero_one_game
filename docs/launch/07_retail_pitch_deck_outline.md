# Point Zero One Digital - Retail Pitch Deck Outline

## Overview
Point Zero One Digital presents a unique opportunity for retail partnerships with our innovative 12-minute financial roguelike game, Sovereign. Our infrastructure is designed to be production-grade and deployment-ready, adhering to strict TypeScript standards with no 'any' usage. All effects in the game are deterministic, ensuring a fair and consistent player experience.

## Non-Negotiables
1. Market Size Data: Provide access to accurate market research data on the gaming industry, specifically focusing on financial roguelike games and mobile gaming markets.
2. TikTok Virality Proof: Demonstrate successful viral marketing campaigns on TikTok that have driven significant user growth for similar games.
3. Unit Economics: Clearly outline the MSRP (Minimum Suggested Retail Price), margin, and turn rate to ensure a profitable partnership for both parties.
4. Placement Ask: Specify preferred placement within retail stores, including digital storefronts, physical shelves, or promotional materials.
5. Marketing Co-op Terms: Define the terms of any marketing co-operation, such as budget allocation, creative control, and performance metrics.
6. Contact and Next Steps: Provide contact information for further discussions and outline the next steps in the partnership process.

## Implementation Spec
1. Market Size Data: We will provide a comprehensive report detailing the current market size of financial roguelike games and mobile gaming, as well as growth projections and trends.
2. TikTok Virality Proof: We will share case studies of successful viral marketing campaigns on TikTok for similar games, including metrics such as reach, engagement, and conversion rates.
3. Unit Economics: Our MSRP is set at $4.99 with a 70/30 revenue split in favor of the retail partner. The turn rate is estimated at 2:1 (two sales per unit).
4. Placement Ask: We request prime placement within digital storefronts and physical shelves, as well as promotional materials such as posters and trailers.
5. Marketing Co-op Terms: We propose a joint marketing budget of $50,000, with creative control shared between both parties. Performance metrics will include user acquisition, retention, and in-app purchases.
6. Contact and Next Steps: For further discussions, please contact [contact_email] and expect a follow-up call within 48 hours to discuss next steps and potential partnership details.

## Edge Cases
1. Market Size Data: In the event that market research data is not available or outdated, we will work with the retail partner to gather and analyze relevant data to inform our strategy.
2. TikTok Virality Proof: If successful viral marketing campaigns on TikTok for similar games are not readily available, we will explore alternative marketing channels and strategies.
3. Unit Economics: In cases where the MSRP or margin needs to be adjusted due to market conditions or retailer requirements, we will work collaboratively to find a mutually beneficial solution.
4. Placement Ask: If preferred placement is not possible, we will negotiate alternative placements that still provide maximum visibility and accessibility for our game.
5. Marketing Co-op Terms: In the event of disagreements on marketing co-op terms, we will engage in open and constructive discussions to find a solution that benefits both parties.
