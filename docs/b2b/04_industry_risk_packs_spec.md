# Industry Risk Pack Specification

## Overview

The Industry Risk Pack is a custom scenario pack designed for specific industries such as healthcare, technology, manufacturing, and education. Each pack aligns with the unique financial risks associated with each industry, providing a tailored gaming experience for our B2B clients.

## Non-Negotiables

1. Strict adherence to Point Zero One Digital's coding standards:
   - TypeScript strict mode
   - No usage of 'any'
   - Production-grade, deployment-ready code
   - Deterministic effects

2. Scenarios must reflect the financial risks inherent to each industry.
3. All packs are subject to approval by Point Zero One Digital's Content Ops team.
4. Engagements for Industry Risk Packs start at $50,000+.

## Implementation Spec

1. Initial consultation: Understanding the client's industry-specific financial risks and tailoring scenarios accordingly.
2. Design phase: Creating custom scenarios that mirror the client's unique financial challenges.
3. Development: Coding the scenarios using TypeScript, ensuring strict mode, no 'any', production-readiness, deterministic effects, and adherence to Point Zero One Digital's coding standards.
4. Testing: Thorough testing of each scenario to ensure functionality and alignment with industry risks.
5. Approval: Submission of the Industry Risk Pack for approval by Point Zero One Digital's Content Ops team.
6. Deployment: Upon approval, the Industry Risk Pack is deployed for client use.

## Edge Cases

1. If a client requires modifications to an existing pack, additional costs may apply and will be discussed during consultation.
2. In case of unforeseen complications during development or testing, Point Zero One Digital will communicate with the client promptly to address any issues.
