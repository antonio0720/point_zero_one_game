# Point Zero One Digital DPA/Security Questionnaire Responses

## Overview

Point Zero One Digital is a game development company specializing in the creation of innovative digital experiences. Our flagship product, a 12-minute financial roguelike game, leverages sovereign infrastructure architect design for production-grade, deployment-ready solutions. All our code adheres to strict TypeScript standards and ensures deterministic effects.

## Non-negotiables

1. **Data Privacy**: We prioritize the protection of personal data in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Our systems are designed to minimize the collection, storage, and processing of sensitive information.

2. **Security**: We implement robust security measures to protect our systems from unauthorized access, data breaches, and other cyber threats. Our infrastructure follows best practices for secure coding, encryption, and regular security audits.

3. **Transparency**: We believe in open communication and are committed to providing clear information about our data handling practices, including our privacy policy and data processing agreements.

## Implementation Spec

1. **Data Collection**: We collect only the minimum amount of personal data necessary for gameplay and account management. All data is stored securely and encrypted at rest.

2. **Data Processing**: We process personal data solely for the purpose of providing our services, and we do not sell or share this data with third parties without explicit consent.

3. **Security Measures**: Our systems employ multi-factor authentication, firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems, and regular vulnerability scans to protect against cyber threats.

4. **Incident Response**: In the event of a security incident, we have established procedures for prompt identification, containment, and resolution, as well as notification of any affected parties in accordance with applicable laws.

## Edge Cases

1. **Third-Party Services**: We may use third-party services for certain aspects of our operations (e.g., cloud storage, analytics). In these cases, we ensure that the service providers adhere to strict data protection and security standards.

2. **Data Retention**: Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected or as required by law. Upon request, we will delete a user's personal data from our systems.
