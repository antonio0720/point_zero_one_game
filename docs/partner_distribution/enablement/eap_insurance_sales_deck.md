# Point Zero One Digital - EAP/Insurance Edition

A 12-minute financial roguelike game for Employee Assistance Programs (EAP) and Insurance providers.

## Overview

Point Zero One Digital offers a unique, production-grade solution designed to enhance your sales strategy and customer engagement. Our game provides an immersive, interactive experience that educates users about financial risk management while promoting your products and services.

## Non-Negotiables

1. **Deterministic Effects**: All in-game effects are predetermined, ensuring fairness and reproducibility of results.
2. **Strict TypeScript**: All code adheres to strict TypeScript mode for improved type safety and maintainability.
3. **Zero 'Any'**: We avoid using the 'any' type in TypeScript to ensure better type checking and fewer runtime errors.
4. **Sovereign Infrastructure Architecture**: Our infrastructure is designed to provide maximum control, security, and scalability for your organization.

## Implementation Spec

1. **Customizable Game Content**: Integrate your branding, products, and services into the game environment.
2. **Risk Literacy Framing**: Educate users about financial risks in an engaging and interactive manner.
3. **Governance**: Implement robust governance mechanisms to ensure fairness, transparency, and compliance with regulatory requirements.
4. **Reporting**: Generate detailed reports on user engagement, performance metrics, and sales leads generated through the game.

## Edge Cases

1. **Multi-language Support**: The game can be localized for multiple languages to cater to a global audience.
2. **Integration with CRM Systems**: Seamless integration with your existing Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems is possible to streamline lead management and sales follow-up processes.
3. **Scalability**: Our infrastructure is designed to scale up or down based on the volume of users, ensuring optimal performance at all times.
