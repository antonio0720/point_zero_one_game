# QBR Template for Point Zero One Digital Partners

## Overview

This QBR (Quarterly Business Review) template is designed to emphasize retention metrics, engagement, cohort results, and the next-season roadmap for our partners. The focus is on providing actionable insights and fostering a collaborative relationship.

## Non-Negotiables

1. **Retention Metrics**: Detailed analysis of user retention rates, churn rates, and lifetime value to assess the health and growth potential of the partnership.
2. **Engagement**: Analysis of user engagement metrics such as session duration, in-app actions, and frequency of use to identify areas for improvement and opportunities for growth.
3. **Cohort Results**: Breakdown of user behavior and performance by cohort to understand trends, patterns, and the impact of specific initiatives on user retention and engagement.
4. **Next-Season Roadmap**: Presentation of our planned features, updates, and strategies for the upcoming quarter, with a focus on addressing identified issues and capitalizing on opportunities.

## Implementation Spec

1. **Data Collection**: Gather data from various sources including in-game analytics, user surveys, and feedback channels.
2. **Analysis**: Analyze the collected data using statistical methods and visualization tools to identify trends, patterns, and insights.
3. **Presentation**: Prepare a clear and concise presentation that highlights key findings, actionable insights, and the next-season roadmap.
4. **Discussion**: Conduct a collaborative discussion with the partner to review the findings, discuss potential strategies, and align on goals for the upcoming quarter.

## Edge Cases

1. **Data Quality**: In cases where data quality is questionable or incomplete, it may be necessary to adjust analysis methods or gather additional data to ensure accurate insights.
2. **Timing**: If data collection or analysis is delayed due to unforeseen circumstances, the QBR may need to be rescheduled or adjusted to accommodate the new timeline.
3. **Partner Feedback**: Incorporate partner feedback into the QBR process to ensure that the review remains relevant and valuable for both parties.
