# Physical Product Specs

## Overview

Point Zero One Digital offers a variety of physical products to complement our digital game, Sovereign. The product line includes the Base Game, Deluxe Edition, Founders Box, Host Kit, and Community Kit.

## Non-Negotiable Standards

1. **Quality**: All products are made with premium materials and adhere to strict quality control standards.
2. **Functionality**: Every product is designed for optimal functionality, ensuring a seamless gaming experience.
3. **Determinism**: Even physical components follow the deterministic principles of Sovereign's codebase.
4. **TypeScript Strict-Mode**: All production-grade TypeScript code is written in strict mode to prevent any unexpected behavior.

## Implementation Spec

### Base Game ($49)
- Deck: 1 standard deck of cards for gameplay.
- Rulebook: A comprehensive guide detailing the rules and mechanics of Sovereign.
- Quick-Start: A simplified guide to help new players get started quickly.

### Deluxe Edition ($99)
- Premium Materials: Upgraded materials for a luxurious gaming experience.
- Expansion Slot: An empty slot for future expansions or additional content.
- Base Game Included: All components from the Base Game are included.

### Founders Box ($149)
- Exclusive Content: Access to exclusive in-game content and benefits.
- Base Game Included: All components from the Base Game are included.

### Host Kit ($299)
- 2 Copies of Sovereign: Two decks, rulebooks, and quick-start guides for hosting events.
- Filming Stand: A sturdy stand for recording gameplay sessions or tutorials.
- Quickcards: Reference cards for easy access to game rules during events.

### Community Kit ($999)
- 10 Copies of Sovereign: Ideal for large events, workshops, or community gatherings.
- Training Materials: Guides and resources for teaching others how to play Sovereign.
- Event Assets: Additional assets to enhance the event experience (posters, banners, etc.).
